COMPUTER SUPPORT SPECIALIST

 

JOB SUMMARY

This position will support personnel using a variety of business applications and computer equipment.  Included are widely used applications such as Microsoft’s Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Access but also specific use business applications. The position will also require general knowledge of personal computers, both desktops and laptops, as well as networking. 

______________________________________________________________________________

 

QUALIFICATIONS

Education/Training/Experience:

Candidates should have a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science with at least two years experience in a computer support position. Candidates with other degrees or certifications such as Microsoft MSCE, MSCA Comptia A+ or Networking+, or with verifiable work experience of two years or greater will be considered.

 

Traits:

1.    Must be organized, detail oriented, manage time effectively and possess effective communication skills both orally and in writing.

2.    Must be a strong team player, self motivated and able to work with minimal supervision.

3.    Must be able to interact with health care professionals.

 

Requirements:

1.    Must possess a valid driver’s license.

2.    Must possess automobile liability insurance according to state law.

3.    Must wear seat belt at all times while operating a motor vehicle.

4.    Dependable transportation must be in good working order.

5.    Must be able to drive an automobile in inclement weather conditions.

 

Physical Demands:

1.    Light work with occasional lifting. Visual acuity and manual dexterity necessary.

2.    Day and overnight travel will be required as necessary.

3.    Sitting, standing and walking is necessary.

 

Risk Category III:

No defined risk for exposure during the performance of this job.

______________________________________________________________________________

 

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

 

1.    Develop spreadsheets that analyze data using pivot tables and graphs from various data sources.

2.    Support personnel using MS Word to create forms and other documents.

3.    Develop high quality presentations using MS PowerPoint.

4.    Support personnel using MS Outlook

5.    Troubleshoot problems with other proprietary business applications.

 

COMPUTER SUPPORT SPECIALIST, CONT’D.

 

6.    Conduct training classes on standard and proprietary business applications.

7.    Install a desktop PC for a user, connect it to a network and instruct on login procedures, security and program access.

8.    Configure a laptop for an end-user, instruct user on the same tasks as above. This will include network configuration and setting up proprietary business software.

9.    Set up network printers and configure PC/Laptops to print to these devices.

10.  Support users by telephone in a manner that is helpful and supportive to solve routine problems.

11.  Conduct training classes on care and use of PC/Laptop to groups.

12.  Provide technical support for Pointcare/HCHB applications and assist with training as needed.

13.  Act as liaison between field staff and IT personnel and provide necessary guidance and  “help desk” support.

14.  Monitor, expedite, and assist in the timely replacement/repair of field devices and provision of appropriate equipment.

15.  Monitor and resolve Horizon’s data collisions in order to preserve integrity of electronic patient care records.

16.  Assist regional and local administrative personnel in procuring and use of electronic media devices to conduct training/inservices/meetings, etc.  Manage issuance and maintenance of media devices for use by agency personnel.

17.  Assist in provision and setting up/troubleshooting equipment for network access in advance of and during meetings.

18.  Assist in testing/developing/troubleshooting/rollout of new Pointcare software.

19.  Provide PRN assistance to the other corps during periods of absence/training of CSS for above functions. Assist/network with other CSSs and IT personnel.

20.  Assists as needed in the orientation and mentoring of new employees.

21.  Attends required meetings and training programs.

22.  Assumes and performs additional duties as indicated.

23.  Serves as a member of various employee committees

______________________________________________________________________________

 

PERSONAL PERFORMANCE

     

1.    Seeks opportunities to serve and respond to needs of others.

2.    Manages workload with minimal unplanned absences.

3.    Reports actual workload, work time and mileage with honesty and integrity and completes entries on a timely basis.

4.    Submits Bright Ideas in support of company pillars of success.

5.    Identifies problems and seeks appropriate resolution to ensure quality care and a cooperative work place.

6.    Demonstrates respectful, professional communication when interacting with patients, families, caregivers, physicians, co-workers and others.
