SaaS Operations Architect/ Manager Data Center Operations

About the Job

My publically traded SaaS Company is seeking a Software-as-a Service (SaaS) Operations Architect/ Manager to architect and build a team to support hosted applications. 

In this position, the successful candidate will be responsible for ensuring that the Company can offer software applications over the web, setting up and architecting the infrastructure to support SaaS applications.  As they begin to build a team, the successful candidate will need to have hands-on participation in setting up the infrastructure.

 

Responsibilities:

·         Design processes and procedures for build-out of SaaS infrastructure

·         Work closely with management on infrastructure improvement projects

·         Design rollout process for server/storage equipment to datacenters;

·         Ensure failover/failback across datacenters;

·         Enhance service level management using monitoring/notification tools and internal processes;

·         Ensure adherence to industry best practices for software lifecycle management, change control, security and regulatory compliance.

·         Provide performance tuning of supported platforms based on resource utilization, monitoring, and desired results;

·         Design Management and configuration of DAS/NAS/SAN storage

·         Provide frequent communications to internal customers and management;

·         Assist in determining team size and skill requirements

·         On-call pager duty

 

 

Required Skills:

 

·         Strong customer service, interpersonal and communication skills (both verbal and written)

·         Excellent problem-solving abilities.

·         A bachelor's degree in computer science, management information systems or related field (or equivalent experience) required

·         Saas Solutions experience

·         Exposure to high-traffic environment (multi-gigabit)

·         Understanding of troubleshooting tools (i.e tcpdump, netstat, iostat, traceroute)

·         High-level knowledge of: HTTP/Apache, DNS/BIND, SSH, SNMP, NFS, Perl and/or Shell, Puppet, Solarwinds Orion

·         Experience with performance tuning, package installation, patch management updates, network & server management issues

·         Experience managing multisite, large scale server farms required

·         You must have experience supporting complex systems in a 24x7 environment

·         Hands-on Management of direct reports


Desired Skills:

  • Experience with SAN/NAS
  • Ability to understand troubleshooting to isolate, diagnose, and correct network problems
  • Knowledge of various network routing protocols: IGRP, RIP, OSPF, BGP
  • Knowledge of MS Windows 2003/2008 Server.
  • Application and Network Security experience
  • Change Control systems and tools (Puppet)
  • Experience in a regulated industry (SOX, HIPAA, FERPA, PCI)
  • Experience working in a follow-the-sun support model.
