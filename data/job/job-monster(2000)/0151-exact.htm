As the prime contractor for D.C. government, MindFinders is seeking three - 3 IT ServUs Desktop Support Tier 1 consultant to start immediately on a long  (10 month) term government contract in Washington, DC.   Please review a job description below.  


If you are interested, please submit resume in a word format with minimum pay requirements to cgreene@themindfinders.com as soon as possible.


Positions:         IT ServUs Desktop Support Tier 1 Technician - 3 positions

Rate:                Up to $24.00 per hour 

Location:          Washington, DC                      


Position Description: 

Provides customer technical phone support by collecting trouble tickets, analyzing issues, carrying out solutions, escalating issues, and tracking to completion.  The preferred candidates must be able to communicate problems/issues to customers in a non-technical manner.  The preferred candidates must have strong customer service and troubleshooting skills.  The candidate must be able to work independently and as a team member.   The candidate must possess at least one of the following certifications:  MCP or MAC 10.X and will be required to obtain certification within 12 months.   In addition, this candidate may be required to work day, evening, holidays and weekend shifts in a 24x7 work environment.  The preferred candidate will be subject to written and scenario based training in both Microsoft and MAC proficiencies.

 Job Requirements include:

  • 2+ years of call center operations experience Tier 1 phone support
  • 1 year of Remedy Call Tracking System or equivalent 
  • 1 year of Remote Control Software
  • 1 year experience with AVAYA ACD Call Routing System or equivalent
  • 1 year experience with MS Office 2003 & 2007 product suite, troubleshooting, etc.
  • Must possess at least one (1) of the following certifications: MCP or MAC 10.X
  • 2 years experience with Microsoft's XP operating system, basic configuring and troubleshooting
  • Basic hardware/Printer/Application support installation & troubleshooting
  • Experience with MAC OS X in a support role
  • Experience with MAC for Office and/or IWorks
  • Hands-on experience w/AD or equivalent for password reset, account creation, etc.
  • Parallels/Boot Camp/Fusion o other VM software
  • Help Desk or other related IT support experience
  • Customers Service skills
  • Basic troubleshooting skills for XP and MAC environment
