Junior Data Analyst

About the Job

Job Summary

This position will utilize analytical thinking, investigative skills, strong Excel, and some MySQL (SQL) skills supporting our eCommerce Data Analytics team.  The Junior Data analyst assists in the preparation of reports for the presentation of data for short and long-term planning. Should have the ability to gather, compile, and analyze data and present reports and summaries in written, tabular, and graphic form. The Junior Data Analyst should be familiar with relational databases (MS SQL) and have strong Excel skills along with conceptual and analytical skills; Needs to be able to plan, design, develop and test macros; Participates in the devising of project timelines, implementation design specifications, documentation, testing and ongoing support of business systems; Develops and maintains operational and system level documentation. Strong communication skills are required as position will interact with business owners. Advanced Excel skills – formatting, graphing, pivot tables, etc preferred. Proficient to strong MySQL/SQL. Solid extraction of data from database, online and offline reports, and other data sources.  Experience in statistical reporting is a plus.  Project management skills a plus.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Support Sales, Marketing, and Business Analysts, Product Managers, and Executive Management with all data analysis and reporting needs.
  • Produce simple to complex reports, primarily from SQL extracts ported to Excel, from web and business data using parameters given.
  • Performs other duties as assigned 


Supervisory Responsibilities:

none

 

Qualifications:

  • Self Starter and able to work independently
  • Analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Interact with users to understand the scope and the particulars of their requests for bug fixes and enhancements
  • Data analysis, data modeling process, modeling and system development life cycle methodologies background
  • Project Management skills
  • Ability to cope with ambiguity and frequently changing priorities
  • Team player; ability to facilitate resolution of issues / changing priorities…find alternative solutions
  • Work well with upper levels
  • Excellent written and communication skills
  • Must work well in a team environment


Special Skills / Qualifications: 

  • 2 years experience with MS SQL Server Database Management System, relational and object oriented data
  • Some Oracle experience is a plus
  • Strong Excel skills 3 to 5 years of experience
  • Excel VBA a plus, but not required
  • Additional skill in PHP/Perl/Java strongly desired, but not required.

College Degree
