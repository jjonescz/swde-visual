About the Job

The Kick-Ass Software Engineer

Profitable, healthcare IT startup looking for a smart developer that knows how to get-stuff-done!

Hello candidate.  Let us first introduce ourselves.  We are Kareo (www.kareo.com), based in Irvine, California.  Our web-based software powers thousands of small medical practices in the U.S.   We’re leading our industry in the shift from traditional software to cloud computing (a.k.a. Software-as-a-Service) and helping doctors get paid by taking today’s complex healthcare system and making it easy.  This allows doctors to spend less time worrying about their business and more time caring for patients and saving lives.  Basically, Kareo is at the center of the storm, which is at the center of Obama’s policy agenda.  Healthcare.  A $2.4 trillion (yes, that’s with a “T”) industry, representing 17% of the U.S. GDP.  Technology is coming to transform and revolutionize this industry and Kareo is positioned to carry the torch for the 500,000 physicians who practice in small medical offices and represent the backbone of the American healthcare system.

That’s the high level.  The details go like this… Kareo, founded in 2005, has recently reached profitability and cash flow positive!  We’ve done this by creating a remarkable product that solves real customer problems, acquiring customers through cost-effective online marketing channels, and keeping a tight rein on expenses. But now we’re generating positive cash flow and we just received a significant investment of growth capital, so it’s time to rapidly grow our business.   Building new revenue-generating features and supporting our growing customer base is going to take a few killer engineers.  You might call this position a Senior Software Engineer, but that doesn’t do it justice.  We’re looking for The Kick-Ass Software Engineer!  Based on your experience, we’re budgeting for an annual salary of $90,000 - $140,000, and you’ll be entitled to benefits and stock options.  Here’s what the position looks like:

We’ll be relying on you to help build the technology that simultaneously creates a euphoric customer experience and enables our business to scale efficiently to tens of thousands of customers.  You’ll be building new features and revenue-generating services like an online patient bill pay, integration with electronic medical records, analytics & benchmarking, and more.  You’ll be enhancing our architecture to support new platforms and enable customization and faster development.  You’ll work with customers to re-design user interfaces to improve the usability of mission-critical business workflows.  You’ll integrate best-of-breed technology to equip our support team to provide legendary customer support via multiple channels including phone, email, online chat, search engines, community knowledge bases, and social media feeds.  You’ll build automated billing and payment systems to provide a predictable stream of cash flow to support the growth of our operations.  But it’s not all glory; you’ll join the rest of the team in fixing bugs, improving performance, and increasing capacity to support the high expectations of our growing, small business customer base.

This is a senior engineering position for Kareo… but make no mistake; you’ll be rolling up your sleeves.  Yes, we are embarking on a substantial growth ramp in terms of revenues and people, but we are still a small startup.  That means you’ll be writing plenty of code and growing with us.  Critical to your success will be a strong foundation in software development, and that means object oriented design, data structures, algorithms, design patterns, and database systems.  You’ll need to hit the ground running so we’re looking for strong technical experience with .NET, C#, WinForms, and ASP.NET with expertise in creating and optimizing SQL Server queries.  But here come the essential ingredients.  We want the smart, hard working, creative problem solver.  Sure, you’re as analytical as they come and you know software design theory, but you’re entrepreneurial and you know when it’s time to stop pontificating and start getting-stuff-done.  You believe the best software is created by small teams that are close to customers and deliver frequent releases in a fast-paced environment.  You bring energy to your work and your passion for software development, and specifically writing code, is infectious.

This killer engineering job is here for the taking but here’s what it’s going to take from you:

  • Send us your resume or a link to your LinkedIn profile.
  • Complete the questions and homework below (this shows us you’re serious). 

 

It’s a lot to ask, so what will we do in return?  If you complete the application, we guarantee that our entire development team will review your application and our Software Engineering Manager will personally respond to you directly within 7 days or less.

 

Kick-ass engineers need-only apply.  What are you waiting for?  We look forward to hearing from you. 


Kareo Kick-Ass Engineer Homework (Required)

1. What would you be doing on a day to day basis in your ideal job?

2. Write pseudo-code (or actual code) for a function that takes in a string and returns the letter that appears the most in that string.


3. Write a WinForms application in .NET using SQL Server as the backend that meets these requirements:
    a. Create a Windows Forms UI with a button
    b. Store a count of how many times the button is pressed in SQL Server
    c. Assume multiple instances of the Windows Form app can run on multiple desktops simultaneously


Zip up and send us all relevant code including table creation scripts with instructions on how to run it. If you don’t have access to Visual Studio or SQL Server feel free to use the free express versions found at http://www.microsoft.com/express/Downloads /.

For confidential consideration, please email your resume AND homework to jobs@kareo.com .
