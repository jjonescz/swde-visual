												<center><strong>Pharmacy - Clinical Resources Specialist<br />St. Paul, MN</strong></center> <p><br /><br /><strong>Smiths Medica</strong>l is a leading supplier of high-quality medical devices and products for global markets. We design and manufacture medical devices in three key areas: Safety Devices, Vital Care and Medication Delivery. <br /><br />Our global customers include hospitals and alternate care, such as home care and other healthcare providers. We employ approximately 7,500 people on six continents and are part of the global technology business Smiths Group, a world leader in the practical application of advanced technologies. We offer a competitive total rewards package and have exciting career opportunities available for forward-thinking, motivated individuals who strive to succeed. Join us as we continue making medical product history! For further information, visit <a href="http://www.smiths-medical.com" target="new"><strong>www.smiths-medical.com</strong></a><br /><br />The successful candidate will provide pharmacy consultative services for internal and external customers that are implementing Smiths Medical devices, disposables, hardware and software. Candidate will offer pharmacy expertise and input for product development and answer customer questions or concerns on related issues. Individual will use pharmacy expertise to provide comprehensive training and support for internal and external customers related to our products to include software applications, disposables, and hardware. In addition, individual will use clinical pharmacy expertise to provide information and guidance on clinical/hospital procedures and drug administration relative to current product use and new product development. Chosen candidate will maintain expert clinical knowledge through review of technical papers and bulletins, workshops/seminars, and interaction with clinical experts.<br /> <br /><strong>Specific responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</strong></p> <ul> <li>Develops and delivers training and provides ongoing support for customer pharmacists on medication software applications for development of custom drug libraries/protocols, collection, and analysis of CQI report data.</li> <li>Facilitates the delivery of the pharmacy component of purchased professional services.</li> <li>Collaborates with Manager of Professional Programs to coordinate coverage for customers using self and pharmacy consultants.</li> <li>Collaborates with Manager of Professional Programs to ensure pharmacy consultants are provided with current product knowledge and updates as available.</li> <li>Content expert for internal new hire training</li> <li>Collaborates and participates in developing expanded service offerings, new product development or product enhancement teams.</li> <li>Consults with RA/QA on issues related to the use and performance of Smiths Medical devices; participates in hazard analysis.</li> <li>This position is required to assure compliance of Company operations to all applicable laws, regulations and standards, good business practices and company documented procedures (including but not limited to FDA, Quality Systems Regulations, ISO 13485, ISO 14001, government occupational health and environmental regulations and statutes).</li> </ul> <p><br /><br /></p>
