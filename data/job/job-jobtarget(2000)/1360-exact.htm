												UnitedHealth Group is an innovative leader in the health and well-being industry, serving more than 55 million Americans. Through our family of companies, we contribute outstanding clinical insight with consumer-friendly services and advanced technology to help people achieve optimal health. What makes your Actuarial career greater with UnitedHealth Group? Advancement, access to data, variety of product lines, and rewards for delivering on our mission ensures you will always be challenged to do your best. The Actuarial Consultant is the subject matter expert and trusted advisor for understanding and interpreting the key drivers of healthcare trends. This individual will be involved in the development of pricing methodologies and assumptions. The Actuarial Consultant will develop program rates, prepare cost estimates, and conduct and evaluate studies on pricing, utilization and healthcare costs. Will work on the development of new product rates and determine the impact on premiums as well as various health product proposals.<BR> Key Areas of Accountability:<BR> 1.Proactively identify, communicate, and lead organizational dialogue related to healthcare cost/trend opportunities and risks throughout the organization. 2.Design and develop sound data-based tools and applications necessary to efficiently measure, monitor, and/or forecast United Healthcare&#39;s medical cost structure. 3.Promote idea generation in support of organizational heathcare cost reduction strategies. 4.Support the Regional medical cost planning and budgeting process. 5.Provide actuarial/analytical support to regional pricing &amp; underwriting teams. 6.Provide management with unbiased advise and opinion regarding medical cost strategy. 7.Build strong inter-dependent relationships within the organization as appropriate, including finance, network pricing, product pricing, underwriting, and clinical operations functional areas. 8.Perform other related projects and analyses as necessary. 9.Select, develop, and appraise staff as appropriate. <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><SPAN STYLE="color:black">Behavioral Requirements: 1.Self-motivated. Effective team player, but works effectively as an individual. 2.Results oriented. Values achievement and pro-actively initiates action. 3.Builds strong cross-functional relationships. 4.Attention to detail and ability to coordinate and manage multiple priorities in fast-paced environment. 5.Coaches, motivates, and leads. Effectively delegates; allows decision-making at the right level.</SPAN></P> <BR> &nbsp; 1. Bachelor&#39;s Degree required, preferably in Mathematics/Finance/Actuarial Science or related field, or an equivalent combination of education and experience. Actuarial designation a plus. 2. 5-8 years of previous experience in actuarial and/or financial data analysis required. Includes at least 4 years of managed healthcare analytics experience or equivalent. 3. 3+ Years group medical claims reserving experience a plus<BR> 4. Experience with the practical uses and applications of healthcare data. 5. Excellent quantitative, problem solving and analytical skills. 6. Strong communication skills, both oral and written. 6. Ability to translate business-driven data needs to technical data/programming team. 8. Ability to effectively communicate complex data/ideas to all levels of management. 9. Strong project management and organizational skills. 10. Strong independent decision-making skills. Converts strategic direction to specific action plans. 11. Proficient spreadsheet skills required; familiarity with Microsoft Access or similar software preferred.<BR> &nbsp;<BR> 12. Candidates must be on the Actuarial exam track, having passed at least 4 exams, near ASA&nbsp; (ASA or FSA designation preferred) UnitedHealthcare is part of the family of companies that make UnitedHealth Group one of the leaders across most major segments of the US health care system. When you work with UnitedHealthcare, what you do matters. It&#39;s that simple... and it&#39;s that challenging. In providing consumer-oriented health benefit plans to millions of people, our goal is to create higher quality care, lower costs and greater access to health care. Join us and you will be empowered to achieve new levels of excellence and make a profound and personal impact as you contribute to new innovations in a vital and complex system. Regardless of your role at UnitedHealthcare, the support you feel all around you will enable you to do what you do with energy, integrity, and confidence. So take the first step in what is sure to be a fast paced and highly diversified career.
