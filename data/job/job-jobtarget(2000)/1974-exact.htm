This position will work with FICO clients providing technical and product assistance to business and technical users. The Product Support Manager will also work internally with Development, Quality Assurance and other groups as needed to provide support to the FICO fraud customer base. <br /><br />Duties of the Product Support Manager include:<br /><br />&bull; Overseeing day-to-day operations for FICO Fraud Product Support.
