									<div id="jt_job_description">
										
											
												<p>The <strong><a href="http://www.gbci.org/" target="_blank">Green Building Certification Institute</a></strong> is currently seeking personable, detail-oriented and highly organized professionals to provide administrative support to the certification team. The certification team at GBCI is responsible for LEED certification and the advancement of the LEED rating systems.</p> <p>The <strong>LEED Certification Associate</strong> will provide efficient and comprehensive administrative support to the certification team at GBCI. This includes data entry and processing, customer service and support, and administrative tasks related to shared technology resources such as network drives, shared email folders, and LEED Online. This associate will also serve as a liaison to the Finance Department with respect to sales orders, invoices, purchase orders, check processing, and other tasks.</p> <p>Individuals with specific language skills strongly preferred: Spanish, French, Italian, Portuguese, Mandarin, and Arabic.</p> <p><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Specific Responsibilities Include</span></strong></p> <p>Primary Responsibilities</p> <ul> <li>Serve as the primary point-of-contact for one or more external review teams, responding to inquiries related to certification reviews and policies, post-application customer service, weekly review capacity, and current review queues</li> <li>Administer GBCI&rsquo;s customized LEED project tracking software, including data entry, database updates, report generation, and workflow tracking</li> <li>Monitor weekly certification metrics, staff review team and QC assignments, and external review team and QC assignments</li> <li>Create purchase orders, sales orders, invoices, promotional codes, check requests, and other accounting documents</li> <li>Track and manage submittals requiring processing outside of LEED Online</li> <li>Maintain up-to-date registered project lists and monitor weekly volume of submittals</li> <li>Monitor certification payment processing and manage invoices and ordering process for third-party vendors</li> <li>Support the certification team staff by managing user profiles and login information in multiple systems</li> <li>Create and maintain stock responses for relevant email templates for LEED Online, payment process, and other tasks as appropriate</li> <li>Manage customer data in GBCI&rsquo;s ERP and CRM software tools</li> <li>Respond to data requests from the certification team, customers, and/or other departments</li> <li>Perform administrative duties for specific LEED rating systems and programs, as necessary</li> <li>Deliver top-quality phone and email customer support, as necessary</li> </ul> <p>Additional responsibilities may include:</p> <ul> <li>Perform quality control (QC) review of work done by external review teams</li> <li>Review project documentation to determine compliance with LEED requirements</li> <li>Assist in conceiving and developing improvements to LEED and the certification review process based on personal experience and vision, or user feedback</li> <li>Tabulate/analyze QC metrics and prepare quality reports</li> <li>Create and compile project team surveys regarding quality issues</li> <li>Prepare training materials and/or reviewer resources with subject matter experts</li> <li>Coordinate/Assemble documents for post-review audits</li> <li>Liaise with the GBCI Technical Customer Service Team, providing technical and process-related support as necessary</li> <li>Schedule, coordinate and manage recurring calls and/or meetings with external review teams, project teams, or GBCI Certification Staff; Record and distribute meeting minutes in a timely manner</li> <li>Create and process scorecards for LEED-certified projects, post to GBCI Web site</li> <li>Ship and receive packages, track all related data</li> <li>Other duties as assigned</li> </ul> <p><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Related Organizational Goals and Objectives</span></strong></p> <ul> <li>Support GBCI&rsquo;s mission to become the premier organization independently recognizing excellence in green building performance and practice globally</li> <li>Encourage and accelerate global adoption of green building practices through LEED standards, tools and performance criteria</li> <li>Offer a comprehensive portfolio of programs including technical tools and services supporting LEED products to meet the diverse needs of the building industry</li> <li>Provide technical expertise to other departments in the organization</li> <li>Streamline LEED certification processes and procedures among consultants and committees</li> </ul> <p><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Education and Training Requirements/Preferences</span></strong></p> <ul> <li>Bachelor&rsquo;s degree in a field of study related to one or more of the following specialties: <ul> <li>Architecture (architecture, landscape architecture, interior design)</li> <li>Engineering (mechanical, civil, structural)</li> <li>Planning (urban/transportation/environmental planning, urban design)</li> <li>Building Operations (facility management, property management)</li> <li>Environmental Science (ecology, biology, environmental management)</li> </ul> </li> <li>LEED Green Associate required (at time of hire or within one year of hire), LEED AP with Specialty preferred</li> <li>Proficiency in various computer systems and IT software strongly preferred (Adobe Lifecycle, Javascript and other scripting language, Microsoft Visio, Office Suite, etc.)</li> <li>Individuals with specific language skills, a plus: <ul> <li>Spanish, French, Italian, Portuguese, Mandarin, and Arabic</li> </ul> </li> </ul> <p><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Experience Requirements</span></strong></p> <ul> <li> Minimum 1 year relevant professional experience working in an office environment</li> </ul> <p><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Other Required Skills and Qualifications</span></strong></p> <ul> <li>Strong computer skills, including proficiency in MS Office suite</li> <li>Knowledge of SAP r/3 and LEED-Online preferred</li> <li>Excellent judgment, analytical thinking, and problem-solving skills</li> <li>Detail-oriented and able to prioritize</li> <li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li> <li>Strong interpersonal and customer service skills</li> <li>Strong sense of personal responsibility and accountability for delivering high quality work</li> <li>Ability to work well both independently and on teams</li> <li>Willingness to self-initiate</li> <li>Passion and commitment to GBCI mission</li> </ul> <p><strong>Application Instructions</strong></p> <p>Compensation includes an excellent benefits package, including 100% employer-paid premiums on healthcare and employer-matching 401(k) program. Please submit resume and letter of interest, <strong>with salary requirements and title of position applying for</strong>, via email to jobs@usgbc.org or fax to 202-478-5046, attn: Human Resources. Please indicate where you saw the posting for this opportunity. <strong>No phone calls please.</strong></p>
