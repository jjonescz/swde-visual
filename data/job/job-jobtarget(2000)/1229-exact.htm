												<p>The Medical Simulation Specialist will assist in organizing and conducting teamwork and train-the-trainer educational courses that use web-based distance learning, social media (e.g. Facebook, Twitter), and on-site clinical simulation technologies (such as mannequins, standardized patients, and computer simulations).&nbsp; The Specialist may be involved in posting content developed by others to blogs and social media, operating web-conferencing computer platforms, coordination of logistics of web-based training, or setting up, troubleshooting and operating mannequin technology for simulation courses or medical device testing, coordinating simulation logistics, and research support.</p> <p><strong>PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:<br /></strong>1.&nbsp;Support faculty and other staff in delivering live clinical simulation training and industry device testing.<br />2.&nbsp;Support faculty and other staff in delivering virtual or web-based training, and social media outreach.<br />3.&nbsp;Support research and administrative activities.</p> <p>Clinical teamwork simulation training and industry device testing duties<br />o&nbsp;Set up, operate, and troubleshoot clinical simulation technologies and associated audio visual equipment so that training courses run smoothly; <br />o&nbsp;Setup and execute speedy transitions of simulation scenarios;<br />o&nbsp;Help systematize and execute ordering and organization of simulation supplies;<br />o&nbsp;Play amateur acting roles during realistic simulation scenarios;<br />o&nbsp;May assist in creating or enhancing simulation props and technologies.</p> <p>Web-based training and education duties<br />o&nbsp;Identify, evaluate, and operate distance learning software, web-conferencing platforms, and social media in consultation with Technical Director and faculty;<br />o&nbsp;Identify, evaluate, operate appropriate distance learning-related hardware and peripherals in consultation with Technical Director.<br />o&nbsp;Assist in the design and creation of audio visual aids in curriculum materials, <br />o&nbsp;Stay abreast of current and emerging education technologies, social media, virtual world and professional communities to help update and refine CMS approaches.<br />o&nbsp;<br />Other duties<br />o&nbsp;May coordinate data collection for research studies including documenting information, collating and assisting in analysis of data<br />o&nbsp;May participate in scheduling and logistical support for all programs held at CMS.<br />o&nbsp;Perform other diverse duties as needed to conduct training sessions, develop simulation or educational technologies, perform research</p>
