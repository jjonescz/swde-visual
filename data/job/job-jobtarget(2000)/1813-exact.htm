												<center><strong>Be part of the team at Smiths Detection, where quality works.</strong></center> <p><br /><br /><strong>Smiths Detection</strong>, a highly successful division of Smiths Group, provides complete security solutions encompassing trace detection equipment, x-ray screening systems and other proven and developing technologies and offers exceptional career opportunities.<br /><br /><span style="color: blue;"><strong>FIELD TECHNICIAN<br />Arlington, VA<br />*Based in the Washington DC Metro Area*</strong></span><br /><br />This position is responsible for providing system support for FirstView&reg; and PROTECT&reg;. FirstView is a web server based video and sensor security software system that is deployed on custom off the shelf (COTS) PC hardware. Support of the system involves deep understanding of PC hardware, and operating systems, and the ability to source and specify PC and other hardware devices to support our systems. This position also requires an understanding of software installation and troubleshooting. The successful candidate would be responsible for loading and validating our software, as well as creating and implementing monitoring procedures to proactively monitor customer systems. The role also involves maintaining good customer relationships by providing professional second level technical support. The role involves helping troubleshoot hardware software and network problems for customers as well as assisting field engineering during installations.<br /><br /><strong>Other responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</strong></p> <ul> <li>Ability to provide remote support over the phone, through remote connections, and onsite support to customers and staff as well. </li> <li>Repair and maintain customer hardware and software as necessary. </li> <li>Assist in maintaining internal IT infrastructure</li> <li>Comply with and ensure department compliance with Company health, safety and environmental policies.</li> <li>Comply with all applicable U.S. export control and security regulations.</li> <li>Perform other duties as assigned </li> </ul>
