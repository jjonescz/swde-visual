The position will act as a business and quality liaison to understand operational compliance requirements and establish how the IT department meets those requirements.<br /> <br /> The position will apply knowledge of IT, applicable regulations and business operations to develop adequate IT policies, procedures and job aids for IT and business personnel and will review and approve quality plans, computerized systems validation and testing projects as necessary. The position will develop internal procedures, establish record keeping methods and practices and provide quality oversight to IT solution delivery and change management processes. The position will have direct supervision of several IT Quality Analysts and indirect supervision of all IT projects in the area of quality.<br /> <br /> The position will ensure that appropriate procedures and quality standards are defined for Release Management and that changes to IT systems are developed and tested in adherence to IT policies, standards and procedures.<br /> <br /> - Liaise with various business groups to understand how they use IT systems in order to assess whether systems should be included within the scope of various compliance areas (FDA, HIPAA, SOX, EU, etc.)<br /> - Provide guidance to service delivery staff and consultants on development of documentation, such as requirements, specifications, test scripts and change control to ensure compliance with applicable regulatory standards and with IT policies and procedures.<br /> - Assist users in understanding the fundamentals of software testing and validation<br /> - Oversee testing and validation activities and assure quality of documentation<br /> - Define the validation strategies for IT and support project teams in implementation<br /> - Approves all system changes and releases back to production.<br /> - Produce various progress reports and metrics</p>
