On the brink of the U.S. nuclear renaissance, we have formed a group capable of high-quality design and construction that provides a single point of contact to the customer. We give the utmost care and strict adherence to safety and regulation requirements.&nbsp; At Zachry Nuclear Engineering, our construction and design teams come together as a collaborative force to create the ideal full-service partner in the nuclear market. <a href="http://www.zhi.com/">www.zhi.com</a></p>
											
										
										
										
											<br><b>Job Requirements</b><br>
											
												<ul> <li>Will perform a variety of engineering and design activities involving nuclear power plant upgrades and/or new power plant projects. </li> <li>Experience with evaluation, selection and application of standard engineering methods, techniques, procedures and criteria. </li> <li>Use independent judgment in performing calculations, modifications and designs and ability to present and justify modification packages to quality and safety reviews. </li> <li>Possess superior technical knowledge of electrical discipline. </li> <li>Ability to work on project teams with other engineers to evaluate conditions, propose modifications and develop design changes which will improve plant performance, safety and reliability. </li> <li>Ability to interface and work with equipment vendors as needed. </li> <li>Experience in reviewing and checking designs, drawings and data prepared by other engineers. </li> </ul> <p><strong>Requirements</strong></p> <ul> <li>BS or MS degree in electrical engineering from an accredited university. </li> <li>PE license is preferred, but not required. </li> <li>Six to ten (6-10) years related work experience with at least 4 of those years with nuclear power experience.&nbsp;&nbsp; </li> <li>Past experience with, and extensive knowledge of nuclear power plant electrical systems and engineering is required. </li> <li>Electrical Distribution System Modeling and calculation experience using ETAP or SKM software is desired. </li> <li>Knowledge of electrical power distribution systems (switchgear, motor control centers, circuit breakers, etc.) and familiarity with electrical load flow, voltage drop and short circuit calculation methodology is required. </li> <li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills as well as presentation skills. Actual field experience on nuclear plant projects is desired.&nbsp;&nbsp; </li> <li>Knowledge of and experience in project scheduling techniques is a plus. </li> <li>Effective time management skills are required as position may involve working on several projects concurrently. </li> <li>Ability to pass security and drug screening, and maintain unescorted nuclear plant access is required. </li> <li>Must be familiar with codes and standards applicable to the nuclear power industry as well as possess working knowledge of applicable state and federal regulations (including the NRC). </li> <li>Superior client skills and understanding of the roles of other disciplines, as well as the ability to coordinate and interface with other disciplines is a must. </li> </ul>
