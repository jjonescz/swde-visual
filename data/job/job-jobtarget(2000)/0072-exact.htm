												<p>Join our dynamic Information Technology team! As an Information Technology Specialist 3, you will analyze, design, develop, and modify software applications in order to respond to changing department needs and technology. The applications you support will give work units the information and tools they need to achieve their goals, which will ultimately contribute to a safer Minnesota. It&rsquo;s all part of working for the State of Minnesota, where you can combine professional growth with personal fulfillment. <br /><br />The Minnesota Department of Corrections is highly regarded nationwide and has provided a model for other corrections systems to follow. Our mission is to contribute to a safer Minnesota by providing core correctional care, changing offender behavior, holding offenders accountable and restoring justice for victims. <br /><br />The Minnesota Department of Corrections &ndash; real people, real careers, a real difference!<br /><br /><strong>Job Duties:</strong><br /><br />Develop and maintain efficient and effective automated information systems in the Department of Corrections' Information Technology unit. <br /><br />Analyze, design, and develop software applications so that clients' operational and informational needs are met. <br /><br />Design, develop, and test enhancements and modifications to existing computerized applications in order to respond to changing department needs and technology. <br /><br />Provide training, documentation, user support, and program maintenance for applications.<br /><br /><strong>Minimum Qualifications:</strong><br /><br />At least 2 years experience developing applications using at least 4 different Microsoft technologies, with a depth of knowledge in each that is sufficient to perform a variety of programming tasks on medium sized projects at the DOC. Technologies may include: .NET 2.0+, VisualBasic.Net, ASP.NET, VB 6.0, ASP Classic, WPF, Web Services, workflows, Silverlight, and LINQ. Other relevant technologies may also be considered. <br /><br />At least 6 months experience with relational database technology including SQL Server and TSQL, sufficient to write queries of moderate complexity. <br /><br />Experience writing or updating reports of medium size and complexity utilizing Crystal Reports or SQL Server Reporting Services. Other relevant reporting technologies may also be considered. <br /><br />Systems analysis understanding and experience sufficient to identify needs and prepare specifications for medium sized change requests. <br /><br />Strong verbal and written communication skills sufficient to clearly document a technical solution, approach, or problem and to work with end users to understand their needs. <br /><br />Experience developing technical documentation. <br /><br />Good understanding of the Software Development Lifecycle. <br /><br />Experience working in a cross-functional team environment. <br /><br />Proven ability to analyze and solve moderately complex computer application and technical problems. <br /><br />Experience working in an information technology environment that consists of client/server applications, web applications, SQL Server, Windows Server, and Windows XP/Vista/Windows 7 Desktops.<br /><br /><strong>Preferred Qualifications:</strong><br /><br />Associate degree or greater in Computer Science, Information Technology or a related field. <br /><br />At least 2 years experience developing applications using at least 4 different Microsoft technologies, with a depth of knowledge in each that is sufficient to perform a variety of programming tasks on large sized projects at the DOC. Technologies may include: .NET 2.0+, VisualBasic.Net, ASP.NET, VB 6.0, ASP Classic, WPF, Web Services, workflows, Silverlight, and LINQ. Other relevant technologies may also be considered. <br /><br />At least 12 months experience with relational database technology including SQL Server and TSQL, sufficient to write queries of moderate complexity. <br /><br />Experience writing or updating reports of large size and complexity utilizing Crystal Reports or SQL Server Reporting Services. Other relevant reporting technologies may also be considered. <br /><br />Experience with developing Web services/WCF. <br /><br />Experience designing and developing new application solutions. <br /><br />Experience maintaining/troubleshooting complex data driven systems. <br /><br />Experience with source code control systems such as Team Foundation Server or Source Safe. <br /><br />Knowledge of the Corrections business. <br /><br />Detailed application knowledge of Minnesota Department of Corrections information technology systems such as COMS.<br /><br /><strong>How to Apply:</strong><br /><br />If you&rsquo;re highly motivated to join our information technology team and contribute to a safer Minnesota, please apply online at <strong>http://www.careers.state.mn.us</strong> and refer to posting #10CORR000432. Contact Kelly Alexander at <strong>kelly.alexander@state.mn.us</strong> with any questions regarding this position. <br /><strong><br />Application Deadline:</strong> Monday, December 6, 2010</p> <p>State of Minnesota<br />AA/EOE</p>
