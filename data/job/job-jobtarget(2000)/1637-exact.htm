HP is a leading global provider of products, technologies, solutions and services to consumers and business. The company's offerings span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services, and imaging and printing. Our $4 billion annual R&D investment fuels the invention of products, solutions and new technologies so we can better serve customers and enter new markets. We invent, engineer and deliver technology solutions that drive business value, create social value and improve the lives of our customers. Hewlett Packard (HP) is the largest IT Company on the planet (over 100B) USD revenue per annum), with a broad product portfolio that spans printing, personal computing, software, services and IT infrastructure.  HP Supply chain IT is currently expanding our logistics practice and is looking for seasoned professionals with a proven track record in the areas of business and it integration in support of an end to end supply chain data flow, with experience in full Systems Development Life Cycle (SDLC) activities.
 
Why join the team at HP Supply chain IT:
  • We are recognized as a global leader in our sector
  • We provide business critical services to our business units and present an excellent opportunity for growth for the right professionals
  • We provide excellent growth opportunities for the right professionals and the ability to achieve new insights and learning opportunities while being part of a world class organization
The position:
Hewlett Packard is seeking people across the United States who can provide immediate support to our current needs and also assist in transforming our supply chain IT landscape.  The right individual will be a self-starter, who can translate their intellectual vision into successful strategic and tactical solutions for HP's internal supply chain. Successful candidates will be required to provide functional expertise in transportation and supply chain domains with proven track record in applying that expertise to a variety of business challenges for the end to end transportation lifecycle.
  • Professional competencies:
  • Leadership skills
  • Customer service
  • Team player
  • Good interpersonal skills
  • Well organized, process oriented
  • Ability to work under pressure and meet tight deadlines
  • Good communication skills
Essential Day-to-Day Responsibilities:
  • Establishes and maintains relationships with key decision-makers at the top of the business, function, geography or organization as well as key tactical leaders. 
  • Uses extensive business knowledge at domain level to drive engagement for the largest IT initiatives. 
  • Proactively engages in strategic business planning where there is IT impact; influences overall business strategies of customer organizations.
  • Communicates strategic IT decisions and plans and works to achieve alignment at the top level of the business, function, or geography.
  • Represents the needs of the business, function, or geography during the IT priority-setting process.
  • Identifies leverage opportunities where IT can provide cost-effective, standard solutions to meet the needs of multiple business/function/geography organizations.
  • Actively participates in IT projects to ensure that business, function, and geography "customers" are included appropriately and ensures that they receive sufficient and timely communications.
  • Promotes best practices found in working with one business/function/geography to other business and function organizations.
  • Identifies the need for new IT processes as well as significant improvements to major IT processes and drives the development and implementation of those.
Educational:
 
Typically a technical Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience and 12 to 15 years of related experience or a Master's degree and a minimum of 10 years of experience.
 
Required Skills:
  • Demonstrable TMS \ WMS design and implementation experience. Oracle OTM package experience is preferred but candidates with comparable experience on other TMS packages like i2, Manhattan, etc. will also be considered.
  • Should have been part of at least 2 full lifecycle implementations for U.S Domestic/International/Multi modal transportation.
  • Five plus years of hands-on functional experience with some or all of the following TMS/WMS capabilities: Ready to Ship, Load planning, Freight Charge Management, Execution and Visibility, Multi Modal Planning.
  • Ability to develop business requirements and turn them into functional and technical designs to be configured/developed by application developers.
  • Work closely with application development organization; work through details of designs, issue resolution, integration, testing and user acceptance sign-off.
  • Development experience with 5+ years of Java experience including J2EE technologies including EJB design and development, servlet experience including popular frameworks, JMS including topics and queues and Object Relational frameworks
  • Design experience with WebLogic
  • Development and design experience with Oracle database including some PL/SQL experience
  • Experience with detailed UML modeling techniques and diagrams; data flow diagrams
  • The ability to work collaboratively in a team environment with both onshore and offshore resource pools
  • A creative approach to problem solving and thought leadership, with the ability to create project artifacts and associated SDLC collateral with minimal guidance
  • Proven negotiation and communication skills, both written and oral;  Ability to control scope and negotiate where necessary
  • Capable of working with multiple business units at all levels
  • The ability to meet deadlines and business unit expectations
  • Ability to create and program manage SIT and UAT phases
  • Ability to work in a team and support less experienced members of the team
  • Required advanced skills:
  • Minimum of 5-7 years TMS experience required, preferably with Oracle Transportation Management (OTM)
  • Experience with data management tools (SQL highly preferred)
  • Experience with ERP applications (SAP highly preferred)
  • Experience in consumer products or related industry as well as experience with parcel, express, multi-weight parcel, air freight, LTL, TL, IMDL and ocean transportation
  • Advanced analytical and problem solving skills and full comprehension of systems development and implementation lifecycle
  • Strong project management and verbal/written communication skills
  • Ability to plan, schedule, work independently and as part of a team
  • Work and communicate effectively with business, IT, and upper management; Prepare presentations for senior managers
  • Ability to apply creative solutions to complex problems and measure the impact to the business
  • Assess issues and the financial impact and cost savings of solutions and project expenses
  • Ability to work under pressure and meet strict deadlines.  Demonstrated ability to handle multiple tasks and assignments simultaneously
  • Strong organization skills, extremely detailed and accurate
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills.  Motivational in tone and spirit.
  • Ability to program manage issues and resolutions
  • PMI preferred but not required
  • Desired skills:
  • Previous development skills utilization (C++, Java, Relational Databases, Scalable multi-tier architecture, data quality analysis); Proven development team lead; leadership
  • Specific supply chain domain expertise on a large scale Fortune 500 (ERP, SAP, Oracle OTM, Manhattan WMS)
  • Background in Data Architecture, Data Integration and Data Modeling
  • Background in Quality Assurance, Testing, Testing methodologies
