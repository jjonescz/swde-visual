												<p><span style="color: blue;"><strong><center>Harper College<br /> Executive Director of Facilities<br /> Palatine, IL</center></strong></span> <br /><br /> Renowned for excellence in teaching and learning, William Rainey Harper College has been enriching lives through learning for more than 40 years. Located in Palatine, IL, Harper is the community college for Chicago's northwest suburbs. <br /><br /> Employees at Harper thrive in a supportive, diverse environment; enjoy excellent benefits; and find inspiration in our ever changing campus setting which features a center for multicultural learning, library, full calendar of cultural events, and a wellness/fitness center. <br /><br /> Harper College is seeking applicants for the <strong>Executive Director of Facilities</strong> position. The Executive Director of Facilities will provide strategic planning, general management, professional direction and leadership for all facilities services/functions including operations and maintenance as well as capital planning, design, and construction for a main campus of 1.2m square feet on 186 acres with 24 buildings, as well as satellite facilities in Prospect Heights and Schaumburg. The successful candidate will be charged with articulating a vision for Facilities Management as well as for developing a clearly defined plan for realizing that vision. The Executive Director leads the Director of Physical Plant, in managing multiple aspects of the day-to-day facilities maintenance and operations of Harper College including custodial services, maintenance, mechanical services and systems, electrical systems, central plant and chilled water operations, elevators, waste management, event support and set-ups, hazardous materials management, engineering, energy conservation, selected outsourced services, and the preservation of the campus landscape.</p>
											
										
										
										
											<br><b>Job Requirements</b><br>
											
												<p><br /><br /> <strong>Requirements: </strong></p> <ul> <li>Bachelor degree in engineering, business administration or related field required. Advanced degree preferred. </li> <li>At least 15 years of facilities related experience in a higher education, health care, or a related corporate facilities environment, with at least 5 of those years serving as a Director. </li> <li>Extensive experience managing a complex facilities operation </li> <li>Superior level of knowledge of the regulatory environment including workplace safety requirements, and environmental regulations and codes </li> <li>Extensive experience in budget preparation and fiscal management as well as solid computing skills including excel and proficiency with project management software </li> <li>Demonstrated knowledge of labor relations and grievance resolution </li> <li>A high level of energy for accomplishing tasks, with timely follow through and strong organizational and scheduling skills </li> <li>A polished, diplomatic, and flexible management style that sets high expectations </li> <li>Comfortable with a fast paced environment where priorities can change rapidly </li> <li>Superb interpersonal, problem solving, presentation and analytical skills along with sound judgment, and the ability to be an advocate for the best interests of the departments within the organization as well as for the College </li> <li>Proven collaborator with the ability to communicate clearly and succinctly both in writing and orally, as well as in one-on-one, small and/or large group settings </li> </ul> <center><em>ALL APPLICANTS MUST APPLY ONLINE</em></center> <center><em>Applications are accepted until the position is filled though priority will be given to applications received on or before November 24, 2010.</em></center> <p><br /><br /> View additional job duties, benefit details and complete our required online application at: <strong> <a href="http://jobs.harpercollege.edu/applicants/Central?quickFind=173909">jobs.harpercollege.edu/applicants/Central?quickFind=173909</a> </strong> <br /><br /> <em>Harper College is an Equal Opportunity Employer.
