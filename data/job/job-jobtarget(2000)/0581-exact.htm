												<b>Job Description: </b>This position is contingent upon contract award.</p><p>ENSCO, Inc. has a requirement for a Senior Software Engineer to support system requirements development, verification and validation on safety-critical control systems. Ideal candidate should possess leadership or participation in multidisciplinary research and collaboration with equipment designers and/or hardware engineers in the design, development, and utilization of electronic data processing systems software; application of research to the planning, design, development, and testing of mechanical and/or electromechanical systems, instruments, controls, engines, and/or machines.</p><p>Initial Tasks:<br>- Assist Customer Control Systems Engineers in the decomposition of engine-level performance and aircraft interface requirements into system level requirements which can be allocated to software.<br>- Assist Control Systems Engineers in developing the traceability from the software high-level requirements to the system requirements, including identification and justification of derived requirements.<br>- Develop Software Design Document (SDD) detailed functional descriptions from Simulink/StateFlow diagrams (low-level requirements and architecture).<br>- Develop traceability from the SDD diagrams to the Software Requirements Documents (SRD) high-level software requirements they implement, and develop additional/remove unnecessary or inaccurate high-level software requirements in the SRD as needed.</p><p>Future Tasks:<br>- Support system integration and verification activities ? test case design, test procedure development and debug, formal test and execution.<br>- System and Software requirements development from higher level functional specifications.<br>- System design activities using Matlab/Simulink/Stateflow or SCADE design tools.<br>- Task and technical project lead role.<br>- May develop and coach others.<br>- Contributes to the business unit by applying their advanced knowledge of one or more functional areas.<br>- Contribute and perform design reviews and other certification documents.<br>- Possible travel to customer sites.<br /><br /><b>Security Clearance Required: </b>NO<br /><br /><b>Qualification Desired: </b>- Experience with DO-178B Level A or SEAL Level 1 standards.<br>- Experience with aircraft engine controls, control law design and/or redundancy management. <br>- Hands-on experience with SCADE, Matlab/Simulink/Stateflow.<br>- Control dynamic design experience.<br>- Knowledgeable about gas turbine engines.<br>- Strong verbal communication skills in French. <br>- Previous experience working with Transport Canada on engine software certification.<br>- Team lead experience.<br /><br /><b>Division Description: </b>ENSCO&#8217;s Avionics Solutions Group provides safety and mission critical systems, software and hardware engineering solutions to the aerospace industry through specific focus on DO-178B, DO-254, DO-160E, SEAL and military standards. </p><p>ENSCO has been delivering avionics solutions since 1983. Our corporation has more than 25 years of avionics systems and software engineering experience and supports all stages of the system development lifecycle including requirements definition and analysis, software and hardware design, development and test, verification, and formal certification. <br>Our process-driven approach and domain expertise offers our customers the confidence they are looking for in an avionics development partner when deploying today&#8217;s high-reliability, safety-critical airborne systems.
											
										
										
										
											<br><b>Job Requirements</b><br>
											
												- Bachelor's degree or equivalent.<br>- Minimum 5 years of closely related experience with design of control law algorithms in commercial or military engine controls or flight controls development projects under DO-178B or SEAL safety assurance levels.<br>- 1-2 years experience in technical leadership.<br>- Experience with the following methods and tools: systems modeling (i.e. Matlab, Simulink, Stateflow, SCADE), requirements management (i.e. DOORs), and configuration change control.<br>- Strong verbal and written communication skills are required and the candidate must be a self-starter and work well in a team environment that is process oriented.<br>- Experience in commercial or military engine or flight control aerospace/avionics applications, preferably, control laws design and/or redundancy management.<br>- Understanding of the technical relationship between system level requirements and software high level requirements.<br>- Experience in developing and supporting application software.<br>- Experience with real-time C/C++ and embedded operating systems. <br>- Experience with avionics communication protocols ? Arinc, 1553, CANbus.<br>- Ability to isolate and debug embedded hardware and software problems.<br>- Experience developing requirements to DO-178B or SEAL standards.<br>- Ability to multi-task and respond to quick turn-around requests ? strong organizational skills.
