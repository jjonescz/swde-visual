About the Job

Network Engineer - CCDA - CCNA

My client, an industry leader, with a benefits package second to none has contacted me to find a full time Network Engineer with a CCDA and CCNA certification and strong design, support and project management experience from a large scale enterprise environment.

 

The Network Engineer is responsible for installing, maintaining, and supporting enterprise-wide communications networks (LAN and WAN) for Information Services. This includes providing very advanced support to ensure information integrity, confidentiality, and smooth network operations to provide maximum performance and availability of data across a large scale multiple-site Healthcare System. The Network Engineer will work in a fast-paced, mission critical environment. Is responsible for the translation of business and customer requirements into solid and stable network infrastructure solutions. Will drive the full technology project life cycle from inception through complete problem resolution, implementation and standardization.

 

Requirements:

CCDA and / or CCNA certification required. Both preferred.

Associate Degree or equivalent training and experience level required.

Bachelor’s degree preferred.

5 to 7 years of hands-on network experience supporting a large networked environment.

Experience in project management including defining requirements, tasks management, resource allocations, and time management.

Experience working with and leading project teams to design and implement new solutions.

Experience working in a team-oriented, collaborative environment.

Flexibility to work in a 24x7 environment oriented towards a zero downtime target.

Able to communicate effectively both orally and in writing.

Ability to document highly complex and detailed technical design.

Able to present to groups and executives effectively in user-friendly language.

Strong interpersonal relationship skills.

Highly motivated having a strong sense of urgency.

Team player with strong organizational skills, a positive attitude and customer service orientation.

Technically strong individual with a breadth of understanding of information management, experience in large-scale integration, and who can embrace a large multi-level system.

Innovative thinker who is able to see the big picture while remaining attentive to the details.

Ability to conduct research into applicable hardware / software issues, standards, and products.

Ability to learn new skills quickly in a fast paced environment.

Keen attention to detail.

Deep knowledge of multiple methods of problem resolution.

Possesses a probing mind and can get to the “root cause” of problem quickly and begin problem resolution.

Understands big picture and intricacies of the work and its affect on customers.

Adept at multi-tasking.

Ability to use rigorous logic and methods to solve very complex problems with effective solutions.

Ability to remain calm, helpful, and productive in all situations – cool, calm and collected.Is cool under pressure.Is seen as the voice of reason during difficult situations.

Exercises sound judgment in making routine, tactical and strategic decisions.

Has foresight and can predict the impact of decisions on other areas of the business or other systems.

Understands health care industry well enough to translate how their work could affect delivery of patient care and financial services.

Is an enterprise thinker who can see the big picture, understands strategic vision and translates it into implementation plans.
