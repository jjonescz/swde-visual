Infrastructure Architect

About the Job

 

COMPANY DESCRIPTION:   Company is an international, full-service business and technology consulting firm focused on shepherding organizations through projects that fundamentally transform their business.  With the experience to create the most ambitious visions, as well as the skills to implement the smallest details of their client’s most critical projects, they have been a proven provider of growth and efficiency to large enterprises, as well as more nimble middle-market organizations.  Their more than 250 consulting professionals drive better business results by harnessing their collective experience across a range of industries, serving clients out of offices across the US and Canada.

 

 

POSITION INFORMATION

 

POSITION TITLE:                     Infrastructure Architect

 

POSITION LOCATION:           Seattle, WA

 

DEPARTMENT:                         Infrastructure

 

DIRECT REPORTS

TO POSITION:                          None

 

 

KEY FUNCTION AND

MAIN RESPONSIBILITIES:

 

  • Client Delivery

 

ü      Thoroughly and accurately analyze information in a systematic fashion

 

ü      Identify roadblocks to collecting and assessing data

 

ü      Assist in the development of recommendations and implementation plans

 

ü      Apply expertise and experience in designing, implementing, migrating and documenting Windows based infrastructures (technologies include Windows, Active Directory, SQL, Exchange, Hyper V, SharePoint, SCCM/SCOM, and Terminal Services)

 

 

·        Practice Development

 

ü      Identify strengths and issues; drive and develop better behavior to create strong consultants

 

ü      Participate actively in the recruiting process for other consultants

 

ü      Drive the development /enhancement of companies methodologies and approaches to client delivery

 

ü      Coach and manage other consultants and actively participate in the performance expectation process

 

 

  • Business Development

 

ü      Participate in business development meetings with prospective clients

 

ü      Understand business needs and requirements and help turn those goals into concrete projects and detailed proposals

 

ü      Create work plans, pricing estimates, and risk assessments for prospects

 

ü      Actively build a professional network and affiliate network in the local community

 

 

III.  POSITION REQUIREMENTS

 

  • A minimum of (4) years of consulting, with a minimum of (1) year of experience architecting complex solution projects

 

  • BS/ Computer Science, Engineering, MIS or equivalent

 

  • Must have experience managing a variety of teams, including small engagements, as well as large initiatives

 

  • Must have strong project management methodology background, including schedule, scope, issue and risk management experience, budget management, change management, strategic planning and analysis

 

  • Must have demonstrated experience managing technical engagements including proposal development, estimation, and day-to-day project management experience (time tracking, budgeting, status reporting, etc.)

 

  • Must have experience working within a fast-paced, collaborative, and team based project environment

 

  • Must have the ability to lead and mentor other team members or developers while leading projects

 

  • Must be able to work with clients and present to C level executives

 

  • Must have a desire to participate in business development and manage client relationships

 

  • Must have experience in developing working plans, project scopes, pricing estimates, and presentations to be able to assist in selling work to clients

 

  • Must have strong analytical, problem solving, and quality experience

 

  • Must have excellent organizational, verbal and written communication skills

 

  • Must thoroughly and accurately understand issues and analyze the problem in a systematic fashion

 

  • Must be able to travel 50% annually

 

  • MCSE or MCITP a plus

 

  • Other expertise desired: VMware, Citrix, Backup tools, anti-virus tools
