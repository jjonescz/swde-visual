Web Designer/Developer

About the Job

 Do you believe that passion and work are two words that naturally belong together? Do you see opportunity in change? Are you committed to providing the customer with a superior experience? If so, you belong here – where the highest standards and exceptional people come together.

 

We are LaCrosse Footwear, Inc. (Nasdaq: BOOT), a leading provider of branded, premium and innovative footwear that keeps lives in motion. Our trusted Danner® and LaCrosse® brands can be found throughout the world at a network of specialty and retail partners.

 

We are currently seeking a Web Designer/Developer to join our LaCrosse Footwear team in Portland, OR.

 

In this position, you will tackle everything from interface design concepts to rock star font-end web development, all with relentless attention to detail and a passion for creating incredible user experiences. This role will be responsible for producing beautiful, simple, intuitive design concepts that consistently represent our brands across multiple projects related to digital marketing, brand engagement, mobile, social, and B2C/B2B ecommerce. This position will also play an instrumental role in front-end web development needed to execute against concepts. If you’re a designer/developer with as much aesthetic aptitude as HTML/CSS/JavaScript know-how, we’d love to chat.

 

WHAT YOU'LL BE DOING

·         Creating design concepts both large and small for projects related to marketing campaigns and branding, ecommerce, mobile, and social media.

·         Working collaboratively with ecommerce, creative, and marketing teams to build interactive design concepts consistent with our brands and business objectives that translate into incredible user experiences.

·         Working with other web developers on executing designs across multiple projects.

 

EXPERIENCE & SKILLS YOU'LL NEED

·         Passion for creating great user experience.

·         Experience creating beautiful, intuitive interactive design concepts.

·         Strong understanding of site structure, navigation, UI principles, wireframes, and user flow.

·         Expert knowledge of front-end web development and underlying technologies including Javascript, CSS, HTML, and AJAX.

·         Experience with back-end web technologies such as PHP.

·         Cross-browser compatibility expertise.

·         Strong collaboration skills.

·         Ability to accept constructive design feedback and quickly adopt design concepts.

·         BA/BS in Computer Science or equivalent experience.
