About the Job


C3G is a fast-growing, information technology staffing and consulting company. Our staff is recognized nationwide for our consultants' expertise, industry knowledge and results-oriented approach. We focus on project management, architects, business analysts, application software development, GIS experts, business intelligence solutions and ERP systems. We have deep industry expertise in healthcare, insurance and energy. Due to our growth we*re looking for talented professionals for full-time consulting opportunities as well as contract, contract-to-hire, and direct hire opportunities.

Currently, we have an opening in the following area:
Code-based Perl developer (5-10 years) with web design to integrate with customer and job board website and frequently communicate with management team and end-users to ensure that product meets user and business requirements, improves website functionality, and enhances the overall design and usability of site. Should be highly analytical and technologically savvy, with strong problem-solving skills. Employer prefers candidates who have expertise with Linux (any variety), MS SQL, Apache Web Server, and AJAX. Require at least a Bachelor's Degree in computer science, mathematics, information systems or software engineering or comparable experience. Job Board Industry experience preferred.

Other Skills
An ideal C3G teammate is bright, self-confident, self-motivated, and results-oriented. Ideal candidates will have a minimum of three years* direct/hands-on experience in their chosen field. Professional certifications are highly desired as is a formal education from an accredited university in computer science or related area of study.
Candidates must also have:
* Strong written and oral communication skills (ability to listen, speak, present, and write)
* Proven client management experience, including ability to organize and disseminate information efficiently and set expectations
* Ability to adapt and work effectively in a variety of configurations * independently, as a member of a team, and in a matrix reporting environment
* Ability to work with all levels of management and build working relationship with others outside of the team
* Ability to multi-task and work with cross-functional teams in a flexible, fast- paced environment
* Excellent time management skills
