Tivoli Netcool Level II Support Engineer - Jr. and Sr. Positions

About the Job

The Level II Tivoli Netcool Support Engineer serves as the customer’s primary point of contact for all communication and provides software support for the IBM Tivoli suite of products as well as products developed and sold by generationE Technologies.  Looking for candidates who are motivated through challenge and opportunities for growth.


The Level II Support Engineer is responsible for the proper troubleshooting, escalation, and documentation of all customer issues, requests, questions, and concerns. This individual maintains ownership of these items from start to finish and is required to provide timely and high quality status updates to customer. The Level II Support Engineer is required to follow documented support processes and procedures and must demonstrate a commitment to customer service in all situations.  

The prime directive of the Level II Support Engineer is customer satisfaction via timely resolution and knowledge of the software. This individual works closely with system administrators, supervisors, and technical staff at the customer site as well as other generationE Technologies support staff to provide successful and timely resolution of support issues.

Two Level II Netcool Positions Available: Jr. Engineer and Senior Engineer.
