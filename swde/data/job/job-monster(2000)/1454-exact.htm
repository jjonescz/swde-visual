Senior Programmer Analyst

About the Job

Title: Senior Programmer Analyst

 

Skills: SAP ABAP/4 + SAP R/3 FI, CO, MM, SD, + Extol + EDI integration support

 

Description:


Haier America is the Sales and Marketing wing of the Americas for the Haier Group based in Qingdao, China.  Headquartered in the Haier Building in New York City, Haier is a leader in room air conditioner, compact refrigerator, and wine cellar sales, as well as a leading force in home appliances, compact laundry, compact freezer and HDTV sales. Haier is an Official Marketing Partner and the Official High Definition Television of the National Basketball Association.


The Senior Programmer Analyst is responsible for the technical analysis, design, programming, testing, debugging and deployment of business applications, reports, interfaces and data conversions of all complexity levels that meet defined business and technical requirements.

Primary Key Result Areas:

Process Analysis: Complete the technical analysis process for development initiatives of all complexity levels resulting in comprehensive programming requirements definitions that meet business and technical needs.

Program Development: Create, maintain, debug and deploy programs of all complexity levels per programming requirements definitions and department standards resulting in business process improvements that increase business efficiencies and effectiveness.

Program Testing: Create program test cases and test scripts per department standards for programs of all complexity levels resulting in well tested programs that are defect free and meet business and technical needs.

Secondary Key Result Areas:

Program Documentation: Create program design documents per department standards for programs of all complexity levels resulting in well documented programs that meet business and technical needs.

System Change Requests: Prioritize and implement manager approved system change requests of all complexity levels by carrying out tasks as required during the full lifecycle of a system change request resulting in process improvements per defined business requirements.

Projects: Perform as a project team member on project implementations; independently completing assigned tasks of all complexity levels resulting in successful, on time and on budget projects.

Teamwork: Provide value as a team member in cross functional implementations while demonstrating interpersonal skills resulting in successful systems implementations.

Requirements:

 

1. At least 5 years of programming work experience with the following is required SAP ABAP/4 Programming Language: SAP programming support for multiple SAP R/3 modules including FI, CO, MM, and SD. Knowledge and experience in the areas of ABAP Workbench, Data Dictionary, SmartForms, SAPScript, Module Pool Programming, Object Oriented programming, SAP BAPIs, User Exit, BAdI, Data Conversion (SAP Standard Load Programs, ALE, LSMW, BDC), ALV & Traditional Report development and Transport & Correction system. SAP development experience within SAP ECC 6.0 is a plus.  Electronic Data Interchange (EDI): EDI integration support for multiple SAP R/3 modules including FI, MM and SD. Skilled in the use of Extol, SAP IDOCs, SAP R/3 EDI configuration and SAP Workflow configuration.

2. Previous participation in a minimum of 1 full life cycle system upgrade or implementation
3. Demonstrated ability to develop effective and innovative systems solutions
4. Strong skills in systems analysis, design, development, testing, and documentation
5. Strong problem solving skills with well organized and structured work habits
6. Excellent verbal, written, and interpersonal communication skills are required
7. A proven ability to function effectively as a team member or independently is required
8. A bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent technical discipline is preferred
