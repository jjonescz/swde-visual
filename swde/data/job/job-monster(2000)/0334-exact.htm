Business Systems Analyst

About the Job

Devon Consulting, leading provider of technical consultants in the area for the past 30 years, has an exciting opportunity with a direct client. Our Client,  a major Financial Company located in Wilmington, DE, is looking to bring on a Business Systems Analyst for a long term contract (12+ mths). 

To be considered for this opportunity, Client is requesting a candidate with AGILE.
 

The Data Services Business Systems Analyst I (DS-BSA I) is responsible for partnering with the business in analysis of process and information needs to clearly identify and document project requirements for technology solutions.  Focus of the role is to build and maintain strong customer relationships, serving as the primary contact-point for the business to engage IT for solution analysis services.  In addition to helping lead in the creation of analysis and requirements definition standards for the organization, the DS - BSA I will also specialize in a specific competency within Requirements Management or Business Systems Analysis and lead the ongoing improvement of that element within the team.


Responsibilities

  • Help to build and maintain close relationships with all business areas 
  • Work as a primary resource for business systems analysis and services for all bank departments 
  • Demonstrate team and departmental leadership 
  • Pro-actively partner with the business in solution identification and requirements definition 
  • Build and maintain a comprehensive knowledge of business processes and technical systems 
  • Participate in establishment of standards for business systems analysis and requirements management 
  • Support bank-wide adoption of business systems analysis services and standards 
  • Apply agile analysis techniques and procedures to document and define system requirements 
  • Utilize functional specifications and prototyping to document system designs


Qualifications

  • Minimum of a bachelors degree from an accredited University, IT-related preferred.
  • At least 6 years combined technical and business analytical experience 
  • Experience in a supervisory or management role 
  • Experience creating and maintaining relationships with business contacts 
  • Ability to conceptualize and think creatively 
  • Excellent interpersonal and relationship building skills 
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills 
  • Excellent problem diagnosis and analytical skills 
  • Excellent organizational skills and ability to balance multiple concurrent efforts 
  • Ability to effectively translate between business and technical language 
  • Ability to lead and mentor a team 
  • Ability to work both independently and as part of a team 
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, demanding, and rapidly changing environment
