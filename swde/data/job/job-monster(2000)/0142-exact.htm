Systems Administrator

About the Job

Concurrent Technologies Corporation (CTC) is committed to assisting industry and government achieve world-class competitiveness.  CTC has a full-time opening for a Systems Administrator at its Johnstown, Pennsylvania location.

 

SYSTEMS ADMINISTRATOR


CTC is seeking a Systems Administrator who will be responsible for resolving support problems and issues related to end-user and enterprise applications.  The successful candidate will lead server and application infrastructure design and implementation efforts; be responsible for resolving technical support problems and issues related to supported applications; and have the ability to diagnose and identify application related problems and effectively communicate solutions to both technical peers and end-users.  The candidate is expected to participate in project-based technology deployments, and in addition, must enhance overall team accomplishments and competence by adhering to project timelines and tracking Service Desk incident resolution through IT Service Management tracking system.  The candidate will provide 24x7 support via an on-call rotation schedule and some travel will be required (<=10%).


The qualified candidate should possess a Bachelor of Arts or Bachelor of Science (B.A. /B.S.) degree in Information Technology, Information Systems, or Computer Science plus 4-6 years of directly related experience.  Excellent written and verbal communication skills are required to effectively participate in a team environment.  Willingness to work independently, accept challenges, and learn new technologies is a must.  The candidate must have experience with Microsoft Windows Server 2008 and Active Directory forest and domain services: Sites and Services, DNS, Group Policy, and DHCP.  The candidate must have experience with Windows Server WAN deployments leveraging RODC, DFS-R, and related services.  Experience with highly available Exchange 2007 environments based on CCR and SCR technologies is also required.  The candidate must excel within the realm of Microsoft operating system applications and be familiar with an array of third-party server applications.  Technologies include, but are not limited to IIS, System Center deployment and monitoring tools (SCCM, SCOM), and VMWare vSphere virtualization.  Experience with Dell blade and standalone sever hardware and NetApp storage technologies is preferred.  Familiarity with ITIL and IT Service Management best practices is a plus.

CTC offers a competitive salary and benefits package and provides professional and personal growth opportunities. Qualified candidates should submit their resumes online at:
 

http://careers.ctc.com

Email: careers@ctc.com
