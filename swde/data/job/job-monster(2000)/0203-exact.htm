Software Maintenance Support Representative, Informatics

About the Job

All applicants MUST apply online at www.waters.com/careers.  Please indicated Monster.com in the referred by section on the online application form.

Requisition #:  10180784

The Software Maintenance Support Representative is responsible for supporting the team’s selling of Informatics contract renewals for SDMS and Creon products.  He or she will be measured against their ability to complete assigned tasks accurately and timely, as well as their contribution to the team’s overall goals for worldwide contract renewals.  Additional responsibilities may include tasks that are an integral part of the Informatics team’s business goals.


Responsibilities

  • Weekly reporting in SAP with reconciliation against orders
  • Uploading data to Informatics customer profiles and updating status
  • Building quotations in excel and SAP
  • Build relationships with other groups within Informatics and other parts of the Waters organization to improve processes which positively impact the business
  • Work towards team goals to achieve worldwide informatics maintenance renewal goals


Qualifications

Education Minimum:

  • Associate degree in either life sciences (chemistry, biology, biochemistry, physics), computer science or business management

    or
  • Minimum 5 years experience in a software and/or science-based organization performing sales or support functions

Advanced

  • Bachelor degree in either life sciences (chemistry, biology, biochemistry, physics), computer science or business management
  • 5+ years experience in a software and/or science-based organization performing sales or support functions
  • Experience with software maintenance contract renewals

Specialized Skills and Knowledge
Minimum

  • Communication skills – both verbal and written
  • Ability to analyze data, interpret data and investigate findings
  • Attention to detail
  • Familiarity with contract renewal processes and/or science based industries
  • Time management, decision-making, presentation and organizational skills
  • Motivated and goal focused
  • Ability to manage multiple projects
  • Team-player: provide team support, feedback, and share responsibilities
  • Computer Skills: Basic applications including Excel, Word, Lotus Notes, SAP

Advanced

  • Telephone sales or phone support
  • Ability to work independently and follow through on tasks
  • Understanding of laboratory operations, data management systems, and instrumentation
  • Experience with a customer database such as SalesLogix, ACT, Onyx or SAP
  • Knowledge of informatics and data management products in the industry
  • Experience in training other colleagues
Approximate % of required travel: <5%.


Waters Corporation is an equal opportunity employer.
