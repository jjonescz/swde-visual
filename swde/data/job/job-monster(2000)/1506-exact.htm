Interaction (UX / UI) Designer

About the Job

General Description (Summary description of the purpose or primary function of the position.)

Data Gathering

·         Conduct stakeholder and user interviews

·         Collect and document design requirements

·         Plan, conduct, and/or participate in user research

·         Create user and behavior models, including personas and scenarios

·         Conduct competitive and/or heuristic analyses

 

Design

·         Architect effective design solutions that meet business and user needs

·         Freely ideate and conceptualize via sketches, concept models, etc.

·         Create functional/interaction specifications

·         Identify and communicate design tradeoffs and alternatives, as needed

·         Present findings, design concepts, and rationale internally and to clients

Skills and Experience

·         BS or BA degree in recognized field

·         5+ years in Information Architecture, Interaction Design, or User Experience role

·         Commitment to the full user-centered design project lifecycle, including user research, requirements gathering, ideation/conceptualization, and design

·         Expertise with standard tools such as Photoshop, Visio, etc. to create visual mockups

·         Experience designing products that combine physical elements and virtual (software) elements

·         Problem solving, resourcefulness, and forward thinking

·         Passion for creating comprehensive and effective design deliverables

·         Refined and compelling presentation skills in varied settings

·         Masters in Information Design, Interaction Design, Information Science, Human-Computer Interaction, or a related field -- or equivalent experience

·         Experience in the K-12 education field, teaching or designing

·         Proficiency with simulation and prototyping tools

·         Industrial design experience

·         Technical depth and understanding of software and hardware development processes

Physical Requirements

·    Ability to articulate clearly and conduct oral presentations.

·    Ability to travel via automobile and/or airplane.

·    Ability to view video display terminal images < 18” away from face for extended period of time – up to four (4)  hours at a time. 

·   Ability to operate a computer keyboard and telephone

·   Ability to sit for extended periods of time – up to four (4) hours at a time.
