Incumbents must be able to perform critical decision making and are given the independence and discretion to set up processes/programs.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span>This position is responsible for administering and conducting municipal elections, organizing and maintaining official documents and public records, preparation of Town Council Agendas, board and commission training, managing the business license process, and ensuring legal compliance of all official postings, public notices, and related advertising. The duties and responsibilities of the Town Clerk are prescribed by Arizona Revised Statutes, the Oro Valley Town Code and the administrative policies and objectives of the Town.</span></p>
											
										
										
										
											<br><b>Job Requirements</b><br>
											
												<p style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;"><strong><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-size: small;">ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</span></span></strong></p> <ul> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-size: small;">Serves as the Town&rsquo;s custodian for public records; maintaining and preserving the public records, permanent documents and legislative history of Mayor and Council and providing public information and access to Town records.</span></span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-size: small;">Serves as Town election official; directs and coordinates Town elections in conformance with applicable local, state and federal laws; coordinates with outside agents to contract for election services; develops policies and procedures to ensure compliance with local, state and federal laws; and, ensures compliance with campaign finance, recall, and referendum state and federal requirements.</span></span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-size: small;">Develops, recommends, and implements new programs, policies, and procedures related to the operations of the Clerk&rsquo;s Office.</span></span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-size: small;">Maintains and attests the official Town documents and records; affirms and signs legal Town documents; maintains the municipal code; supervises the preparation and codification of code updates; and administers oaths of office.</span></span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-size: small;">Responds to requests from information from employees, managers, elected officials and the general public.</span></span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-size: small;">Supports council and management activities by directing the preparation and posting of all meetings, recording documents, researching new technologies, working with elected officials, departments and employees, attending meetings, and responding to inquiries and requests.</span></span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-size: small;">Manages the business license and registration program; develops policies and procedures which govern the application and issuance of business licenses and registrations; and interprets codes and explains requirements to staff, applicants, and/or the general public.</span></span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-size: small;">Accepts lawsuits and claims served on the Town.</span></span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-size: small;">Administers the processing of annexation documents in accordance with state law.</span></span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-size: small;">Provides orientation sessions for newly appointed Board/Commission members with the Town Attorney; prepares and presents oral and written reports to Council and staff.</span></span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-size: small;">Coordinates and oversees the preparation and distribution of Town Council agendas and supporting materials for Council meetings; attends meetings of the Town Council; provides follow-up on actions directed or taken by Council.</span></span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-size: small;">Supervises Clerk&rsquo;s staff and ensures the provision of customer service in a professional, courteous and timely manner; conducts performance appraisals.</span></span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-size: small;">Manages the development and implementation of the Department&rsquo;s goals, objectives, policies, procedures, and work standards.</span></span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-size: small;">Manages staff by planning and prioritizing tasks, ensuring policy and procedure compliance, recommending changes and adjustments, coordinating efforts with departments, ensuring legal compliance, and monitoring staff performance and development.</span></span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-size: small;">Manages department&rsquo;s financial resources; researches, develops and administers the department&rsquo;s budget; monitors expenditures throughout the fiscal year; reviews and approves financial documents; and forecasts future department<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span>resources needs.</span></span></li> </ul> <p><span style="font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-size: small;"> <p style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;"><strong><span style="font-family: Arial;">REQUIRED <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp;</span>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES:</span></strong></p> <ul> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;">Extensive knowledge of the municipal clerk&rsquo;s office.</span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;">Knowledge of State Constitution and State Statutes related to annexation, elections, open meetings, records management, and Town Codes relating to elections, annexations, and all activities of the Town Clerk&rsquo;s Office.</span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;">Knowledge of management and/or supervision principles.</span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;">Knowledge of office and records management and workflow principles and procedures.</span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;">Knowledge of procedures for conducting public meetings and parliamentary procedures.</span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;">Knowledge of on-line streaming systems and automated agenda software systems.</span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;">Skill in supervising, delegating and evaluating work of subordinate staff.</span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;">Knowledge of customer service principles and practices; commitment to a high standard of customer orientation and service.</span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;">Skill in oral and written communication and comfortable with public presentation responsibilities.</span></li> <li><span style="font-family: Arial;">Skill in operating a personal computer, standard office equipment;
