												<p>Director of Recruiting</p> <p style="text-align: left;"><br /><strong>Company Overview</strong> <br />A software company focused on providing reliable, cost-effective Business Intelligence software solutions to clients within both the public and private sectors. The Company areas of experience and expertise include&nbsp;Intelligent Software&nbsp;and Professional Services. <br /><br /><strong>Description <br /></strong>The Director of Recruiting supports the company recruiting process and will be required to build a scalable repeatable recruiting model using focused metrics and accountability. This includes identifying and attracting qualified candidates through the processes of sourcing, interviewing, and preparing candidates for the selection and hiring stages. The Director of Recruiting establishes enterprise recruiting tools, programs, systems and resources. <br /><br /><strong>Educational / Work Experience&nbsp;<br /><br /><em>&nbsp;Candidate must be at Director Level of Recruiting and have Professional Services experience</em></strong></p> <ul> <li>Develop the company recruiting plan, recruiting vision statement and recruiting objectives and be held accountable for attaining those objectives, Monitor and update the plan as necessary</li> <li>Four-year degree, graduate degree preferred </li> <li>8-10 years experience in recruiting management and leadership </li> <li>Lead a team of recruiting managers and recruiters </li> <li>Develop measurement systems to assess the effectiveness of recruiting efforts (metrics) </li> <li>Responsible for talent acquisition reporting, trend analysis and reporting of metrics by period </li> <li>Evaluate and assess effectiveness of recruiting strategies and talent retention systems to continuously improve talent acquisition efforts </li> <li>Identify and source candidates. </li> <li>Coordinate candidate recruiting and establish candidate pools. Meet company growth target and support AAP and EEO initiatives. </li> <li>Effectively partner with other departments by providing ongoing communication on projects, performance and process </li> <li>Properly utilize/create company processes including selection tests and interview guide to screen candidates. </li> <li>Solicit internal referrals. </li> <li>Keep employees constantly aware of the company recruiting need by leveraging the referral programs provided by the company. </li> <li>Report on recruiting activities and results to management team by conducting regular recruiting update meetings. </li> <li>Act as a source for the recruiting process to hiring managers, candidates, applicants, and employees. </li> <li>Other duties as assigned by senior management. </li> </ul>
