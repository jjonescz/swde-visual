He/she will apply analytical rigor to all work but remain open to non traditional information or conclusions.&nbsp; The analyst will have excellent writing and presentation skills and be able to defend his/her analytical conclusions in logical and persuasive terms. The Analyst will be able to work to tight deadlines and be able to function calmly and efficiently in crisis situations.&nbsp;</div> <div>&nbsp;</div> <div><strong>Responsibilities</strong></div> <div> <ul> <li>Provide cogent intelligence analysis to PG&amp;E decision makers in both written and oral form </li> <li>Maintain a high state of situational awareness regarding threats to the Corporation, the industry and US critical infrastructure </li> <li>Initiate pertinent analysis in accordance with established intelligence priorities </li> <li>Alert decision makers to emerging threats in a timely manner </li> <li>Identify, vet and develop new information sources </li> <li>Leverage existing expertise and knowledge in order to improve the intelligence deliverable </li> <li>Leverage and expand the capabilities of existing analytical tools and technologies; recommend new technologies as appropriate </li> <li>Support crisis events with intelligence collection, analysis and/or dissemination</li> </ul> </div> <div>&nbsp;</div>
