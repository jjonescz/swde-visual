												<p><strong>Department Head - Information Services</strong></p> <p><strong>MIT Lincoln Laboratory, Lexington, MA</strong></p> <p>MIT Lincoln Laboratory, headquartered in Lexington, MA, is a not-for-profit, Federally Funded Research and Development Center operated by the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. The Laboratory conducts research and development in the area of advanced electronics for the Department of Defense and other Government agencies, has an annual budget of approximately $850M, and employs a staff of 3000 plus 500 subcontractors.</p> <p>The Laboratory is currently seeking a qualified candidate, accomplished in strategic planning, to lead Information Services for the entire organization. This individual will provide direct supervision to six functional supervisors/managers and will assume overall management responsibility for the Information Services Department, which includes 190 employees and has an annual budget of $30M. Working with senior management, s/he will be responsible for the planning, development, and delivery of information technology and services to meet Laboratory requirements and objectives including the implementation of an established IT road map. Other key areas of responsibility include effective utilization of the SAP Enterprise Resource Planning system, IT Security (in partnership with the Security Services Department), IT systems design and analysis, voice and data networks, systems infrastructure, systems programming, application development, research library and laboratory archives, and creative multimedia design services. The successful candidate will:</p> <ul> <li>Ensure the effective and efficient delivery of information services to the Laboratory's research and administrative staffs.</li> <li>Research and evaluate IT systems, policies, programs and procedures to support research and administration objectives. Evaluate and propose costs and schedules for IT requirements and projects.</li> <li>Develop and recommend IT governance policies and strategies to achieve maximum efficiencies and security of all resources. Will develop and implement budget strategies for cost containment and accountability.</li> <li>Develop an understanding of the staff's workflow and proactively look to simplify and enhance the Laboratory's IT structure to increase productivity.</li> <li>Develop and implement IT training programs for department staff and the Laboratory community.</li> <li>Manage and develop department staff. Ensure continuous commitment to improved customer service delivery along with the development of methodologies to manage the service lifecycle. </li> <li>Manage the Laboratory's IT Disaster Recovery/Business Continuity Plan. Evaluates proposed changes and expansion of available services.</li> <li>Define and adjust IT standards and technologies.</li> <li>Oversee the development, design, and implementation of new applications and changes to existing computer systems and software packages. </li> <li>Develop and support Laboratory information research needs by delivering information and information service products through content creation, user experience design, information discovery, knowledge management, and intellectual property preservation to customers. </li> <li>Maintain the stability of current operations and ensure stability of operations as new technologies are delivered.</li> <li>Maintain current knowledge of government, industry, and university research IT trends, and ensure procedural and regulatory compliance.</li> <li>Perform other duties and delivers special projects and studies as assigned by the executive management team.</li> </ul>
											
										
										
										
											<br><b>Job Requirements</b><br>
											
												<p><strong>Degrees and Experience:</strong></p> <ul> <li>MS in Computer Science or MBA or equivalent business experience and 10+ years of applicable experience. Previous experience in a federal, educational, research or not-for-profit setting preferred.</li> <li>Demonstrated accomplishment in the delivery of customer service to technical research, scientific, engineering, and administrative staff.</li> <li>Demonstrated experience in large scale IT projects that are collaborative efforts in design and implementation including a deep understanding of best practices in identifying and addressing stakeholder information needs.</li> <li>Experience that includes exposure to shared and outsourced solutions as well as support of in-house systems in a federated multi-site client server environment.</li> <li>Demonstrated record of IT management experience, continued growth, and career success within an engineering and technology development environment, where technical staff are key stakeholders in the IT services, is desired.</li> <li>Experience in managing ERP systems is required; SAP experience is desired.</li> <li>Demonstrated ability to develop, track, and control IT budgets effectively and efficiently.</li> </ul> <p><strong>Skills:</strong></p> <ul> <li>Demonstrated ability to provide motivational leadership for staff at all levels.</li> <li>Passion for moving an organization forward, but patient and thoughtful enough to deal with budgetary and political realities inside a complex organization. </li> <li>Proven experience communicating department progress and managing department relationships in all levels of the organization, including demonstrated ability to translate information services language into user-friendly language. </li> <li>Comfortable working in a highly collaborative environment and leading through influence as opposed to leading through a command and control style.</li> <li>Accomplished at problem analysis and resolution.</li> </ul> <p><strong>For more information about the Department Head - Information Services position (Req # 2383) and the Laboratory's many other opportunities, and to apply, please visit our website at <a href="http://www.ll.mit.edu/" title="blocked::http://www.ll.mit.edu/">www.ll.mit.edu</a>.</strong></p> <p>As an Equal Opportunity Employer, we are committed to realizing our vision of inclusion in every aspect of our enterprise. Due to the unique nature of our work, we require U.S. citizenship.</p>
