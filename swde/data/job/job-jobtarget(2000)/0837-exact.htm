												<p>The Cook County Health &amp; Hospitals System (CCHHS) is seeking a <strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">System Privacy Officer</span></strong> to manage the ongoing development, implementation, maintenance, and evolution of CCHHS' privacy program and all related privacy activities. <br /> <p>CCHHS is a publicly funded, safety-net healthcare system comprised of three (3) hospitals, John H. Stroger Jr. Hospital, Provident Hospital, and Oak Forest Hospital, the Cook County Department of Public Health, Cermak Health Services in the Cook County Jail and Temporary Juvenile Detention Center, the Ruth Rothstein CORE Center and sixteen (16) Ambulatory and Community Health Network (ACHN) clinics.&nbsp; <br /> <p>The System Privacy Officer will reflect the mission and vision of CCHHS, adhere to the organization's Standard of Conduct and Corporate Compliance Program, and comply with all relevant policies, procedures, guidelines and all other regulatory and accreditation standards.</p> <p><strong>RESPONSIBILITIES:</strong></p> <ul> <li>Serves in a leadership role to develop a system-wide privacy program as part of the system's compliance program.</li> <li>Synchronizes system-wide privacy program materials and messaging to present a uniform message.</li> <li>Develops strong system-wide practices through privacy policies and procedures.</li> <li>Assesses, evaluates, implements, maintains, and updates CCHHS information privacy policies and procedures.</li> <li>Designs system-wide plans for auditing and monitoring.</li> <li>Establishes a structured process for regulatory review, monitoring, and dissemination of information. Modifies policies, procedures, and projects to reflect changes in laws and regulations.</li> <li>Develops and coordinates privacy projects with CCHHS system entities.</li> <li>Oversees, directs, delivers, or ensures delivery of privacy education, training and orientation to all senior leadership, employees, physicians, volunteers, students, vendors, and consultants.</li> <li>Establishes and administers a process for receiving, documenting, tracking, investigating, and taking action on all privacy complaints. Collaborates with operational areas to remediate concerns.</li> <li>&nbsp;Maintains highest levels of confidentiality regarding all departmental operations - in communication both verbal and written and with the use of technology.</li> </ul> <p><br /><em><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Additional Functions</span></em>&nbsp;</p> <ul> <li>Composes and prepares complete and accurate privacy material for the compliance program (material is defined but not limited to the following examples: documents, memorandum, meeting minutes and agendas, narrative and statistical reports, presentations, projects, overheads, slides, graphs, charts, and listings) within designated time parameters. </li> <li>Ability to prioritize work with minimal supervision and use time effectively.&nbsp; Requires high degree of follow-through despite frequent interruptions.&nbsp; Possess ability to work in a fast-paced environment, which requires handling multiple tasks at once.</li> </ul> </p> </p>
											
										
										
										
											<br><b>Job Requirements</b><br>
											
												<p><strong>EDUCATION/EXPERIENCE QUALIFICATIONS:</strong></p> <ul> <li>Three+ (3+) years recent experience in HIPAA privacy and/or security including program analysis, development and testing required</li> <li>Three+ (3+) years recent experience in a hospital setting or large multi-specialty physician group practice required</li> <li>Three+ (3+) years of conducting complex healthcare privacy investigations</li> <li>Leadership competencies to include planning and organizing, problem solving, informing, consulting, supporting and networking</li> <li>Project Management experience and exceptional organizational skills</li> <li>Extensive development experience for reports &amp; Policy / Procedure </li> <li>Knowledge of Health Care Regulatory standards and accreditation agencies, especially those affecting privacy and security</li> <li>Experience in Public Speaking and Business Presentations</li> <li>Highly developed software application skills - MS Office Access, Excel, PowerPoint, and Word</li> <li>Behavioral commitment to quality work and customer service philosophy</li> <li>Strong communication skills - written and verbal; skilled in partnership development and conflict resolution</li> <li>Preferred: Masters Degree in Healthcare, Business, Education, or related field or Juris Doctor (JD)</li> </ul> <p style="text-align: justify; margin: 0in 0in 0pt;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Arial Narrow&quot;; font-size: 12pt;">CCHHS is an equal opportunity and affirmative action employer committed to a drug-free and smoke-free workplace.</span></p>
