												<center><strong>Be part of the team at Smiths Detection, where quality works.</strong></center> <p><br /><br />Smiths Detection, a highly successful division of Smiths Group, is doing work that matters to countless individuals worldwide. In addition to complete security solutions encompassing trace detection equipment, x-ray screening systems and other proven and developing technologies, we offer incredible career opportunities.</p> <center><strong>Product Manager<br />Danbury, CT </strong></center> <p><br />The purpose of this job is to manage the lifecycle of one or more products in order to achieve the maximum revenue, market share, profit, ROI and customer satisfaction while being consistent with the Company's overall strategic business plan. A Product Manager is responsible for the one or more of the Company's most strategically and financially important products or product lines. Product Management is part of Marketing and therefore belongs to or works closely with Marketing departments.<br /><br /><strong>SPECIFIC RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE, BUT ARE NOT LIMITED TO:</strong></p> <li> Responsible for the overall management of the lifecycle for one or more products, through concept, planning, development, product introduction, growth, maturity, decline, and planned obsolescence.</li> <li> Leads the marketing research effort by collecting information about the market, customers and competitive products as well as the chances and risks of market developments.</li> <li> Based on market research, technical and commercial feasibility studies and business cases, develops and decides on product concepts. Presents concepts to senior management for approval of product development.</li> <li> Prepares a product development road map to manage the transition and coordination of the product among a cross functional team as the product development moves from R&amp;D, to Engineering, to Manufacturing and Supply Chain, to delivery. </li> <li> Provides regular status reports to senior management, customers, and suppliers.</li> <li> Determines if beta testing or product pilot testing is required, oversees testing and uses results to make any necessary product modifications. </li> <li> Initiates market introduction together with Marketing Communications and advises on how to best position and sell product, including recommended selling prices. Gives product demonstrations to customers, and at trade shows as needed in support of Sales. Supports Sales efforts by providing sales kits, product overviews and demonstrations, technical reference materials, and training sales team.</li> <li> Follows up on success of the product and manages the lifecycle coordinating necessary product changes and upgrades and prepares for product obsolescence.</li> <li> During the product's life cycle, looks for opportunities to create a portfolio or family of related products to sell to additional market segments at both lower and higher price points without eroding the market share. Coordinates development of platforms and seeks to use synergies e.g. re-using sub-assemblies and components.</li> <li> Prepares and implements product's exit plan including the future use of subassemblies and components.</li> <li> Works with Sales to maintain relationship with customers to proactively resolve any issues regarding product quality and/or service, and to proactively plan product enhancements and future generations. </li> <li> Conducts product evaluations, document results of evaluations and inform and advise other departments about product modifications and/or future generations based on the results of the evaluations.</li> <li> Comply with and ensure department compliance with Company health, safety and environmental policies.</li> <li> Comply with all applicable U.S. export control and security regulations.</li> <li> Other duties as required.</li>
