									<div id="jt_job_description">
										
											
												<p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 11pt;">As a key member of the core leadership team for University Financial Aid, the Director of Financial Aid Operations provides technical, operational and system, as well as managerial leadership to all financial aid offices in the mission critical area of financial aid operational and processing services. Reporting to the University Director of Financial Aid, the Director is relied upon as a business process expert and coordinator among Columbia University financial aid offices in all aspects of the various functional requirements executed through PowerFAIDs and through the Student Information Systems. Specific responsibilities include the following: Responsible for external data loads, system data exchanges and related issues resolutions; leads operations, testing, and system support for financial aid regulatory and software updates; provides troubleshooting, problem resolution, and business and process analysis to resolve issues and to improve administrative financial aid processes; provides operational support to financial aid offices to ensure compliant and expeditious processing of financial aid; serves as IT liaison for accomplishment of enhancement requests and approved projects as related to external updates, custom data needs, and reporting requirements; as manager for several direct reports, provides leadership interaction with SIS/SSOL programming team, participates in the Columbia University Financial Aid Community user group initiatives and cross-University Financial Aid user group, and fills in for the University Director of Financial Aid when required; provides system support for ELM, CSS Profile, Department of Education EDconnect, COD; and performs other duties as assigned.</span></p>

Minimum of eight years&rsquo; experience within the student administrative life-cycle working with student information systems, or similar enterprise data systems within a complex business environment. </span></p> <p style="text-align: justify;"><strong><span style="font-size: 11pt;">Preferred Qualifications:</span></strong></p> <p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 11pt;">Demonstrated excellence in leading collaborative information systems projects in environments with diverse interests and multiple distributed operations.
