From the camaraderie you share with your coworkers to our commitment to the continued training and development of our team, we work hard to make you feel like you are part of something special. <br /><br /> We are currently seeking a qualified individual to fill the role of <strong>Training Manager</strong> at our new <strong>Evans, CO</strong> location. <br /><br /> The Training Manager will plan, develop, and manage activities associated with the training and development of employees within company guidelines and client expectations. The incumbent will maintain a climate of customer oriented service excellence and increasing performance through application of quality tools and processes. <br /><br /> Supervisor responsibilities include: interviewing, hiring, and developing staff; planning, assigning, and directing work; appraising performance; certifying trainers; rewarding and disciplining employees; addressing complaints and resolving problems. <br /><br /> Essential Functions and Responsibilities:</p> <li>Partner with Operations Management to provide performance feedback (individual, client, and organizational) and develop solutions that ensure requirements are exceeded. Identify performance opportunities and implement measurable solutions to ensure performance standards and customer expectations are exceeded. </li> <li>Ensure consistent program development, delivery, and validation of training programs through systematic training and quality approach (new/existing client training, system/process change training, procedural changes, and continuous improvement activities). Collaboratively work across multiple centers and with Operations and Client Management teams to ensure value added solutions meet our client's expectations. </li> <li>Create and Develop High Performing Training Teams. Evaluate, coach and develop Instructors to achieve the highest levels of success within the Organization. Compile, review, and monitor performance of Instructors' effectiveness. </li> <li>Work with Workforce Administration, Operations Management, and HR Teams to plan and execute training activities. Meet frequently to track progress, adjust plans, and provide performance feedback that may impact plans. </li> <li>Implement motivational programs to ensure higher levels of performance. Attend scheduled training/business growth seminars and training meetings as scheduled.</li>

Preferable experience would include projects that are local or enterprise-wide. </li> <li>Highly proficient in communication, planning, leadership, coordinating, and coaching is preferred. </li> <li>Working knowledge and experience using structured problem solving process and tools (such as six sigma or total quality management) is preferred. </li> <li>Experience in conflict resolution/negotiation, team and consensus building is preferred. </li> <li>Proficient in MS Office, flowcharting, and project management software is preferred. <br /><br /> We offer an attractive compensation package along with exceptional career potential in a dynamic leading-edge enterprise. <br /><br /> For consideration, please apply online at: <strong><a href="http://afnicareers.com" target="new">afnicareers.com</a></strong> or send your resume to <strong><a href="mailto:recruiter@afni.com ">recruiter@afni.com</a></strong> and specify position title in the subject line. <br /><br /> <center>Afni, Inc.<br /> <br /><br /> Equal Opportunity Employer </center></li>
