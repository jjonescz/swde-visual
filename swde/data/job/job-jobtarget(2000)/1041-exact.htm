We are a health-care system that fosters a positive environment, a celebration of the individual, and your skills and contributions. Serving San Diego's inland North County communities for more than 50 years, PPH is a family of outstanding healthcare facilities that cover an area of more than 850-square miles. With a full spectrum of health services, our state-of-the-art hospitals, birth centers, rehabilitation and long-term care centers, plus home health care and hospice services, PPH delivers health care that meets the needs of every member in our community.</span></p> <p><span style="font-size: small;">We have an excellent opportunity for an experienced <strong>Bariatric Clinical Coordinator. </strong>The goal of the Bariatric Clinical Coordinator is to optimize Palomar Pomerado Health (PPH) bariatric patient outcomes and improve the quality of life through clinical and educational expertise and support. This is achieved by the Bariatric Clinical Coordinator position being primarily accountable for developing and coordinating the PPH daily clinical operations and maintenance of the Bariatric Surgery Center of Excellence (BSCOE) in accordance with the standards and mandates of the American Surgery for Metabolic and Bariatric Surgery (ASMBS) body under the surveillance of the Surgical Review Corporation (SRC).</span></p> <p><span style="font-size: small;"><strong>These requirements are met through:</strong><br /></span></p> <ul> <li><span style="font-size: small;">Administrative equipment and resources, ensuring the development, revision and implementation of system wide related bariatric surgery education for PPH personnel, maintaining records of required credentialing, clinical pathways and standardized orders, ensuring joint facility and practice data reporting utilizing appropriate data software applications, as well as compliance with any further mandated standards and requirements. <br /></span></li> <li><span style="font-size: small;">Serves as the clinical liaison for PPH between facility and practice/surgeon clinical care areas to ensure for an optimum patient experience in every case, and that all requirements are met for joint compliant status of the BSCOE as applicable to the facility and practice/surgeon. <br /></span></li> <li><span style="font-size: small;">Continuously assesses the clinical operations and outcomes, developing, professionally presenting and implementing approved recommendations for PPH to not only meet the objectives, and standards. <br /></span></li> <li><span style="font-size: small;">Collaborates with the PPH Bariatric Program Manager for the development of clinical aspects related to program growth and for promoting teamwork amongst PPH clinical care and continuum of care areas, and between PPH and practice clinical care areas. <br /></span></li> <li><span style="font-size: small;">Performs duties where competent in any nursing capacity. <br /></span></li> <li><span style="font-size: small;">Understands and adheres to the California Nurse Practice Act, The American Nurses Association (ANA) Code of Ethics for Nurses, the ANA Bill of Rights for Registered Nurses and the ANA Scope and Standards of Practice for Nursing. <br /></span></li> <li><span style="font-size: small;">Performs other duties as assigned. <br /></span></li> <li><span style="font-size: small;">Follows PPH rules, policies, procedures and applicable laws, and regulatory standards. <br /></span></li> <li><span style="font-size: small;">Carries out the mission, vision, values and quality commitment of PPH.</span></li> </ul>
