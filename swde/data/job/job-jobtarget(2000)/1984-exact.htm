												<p><strong>Purpose of Role:</strong></p> <p>The web development manager will lead our development team of contractors and vendors to design &amp; deliver a leading edge web environment strategy for Mars Symbioscience businesses, while still being able to operate tactically at times to ensure things are being built from the ground up in line with business requirements. This role will be responsible for managing, supporting and optimizing existing systems as well as building out new systems utilizing leading edge technology and online business practices. The ideal candidate for this role will have a solid technical background, a proven background of implementing new technologies to drive business objectives, as well as a proven ability to manage complex projects from start to finish.</p> <p><strong>Principal Accountabilities</strong></p> <ul> <li>Manage the build out and support of our ecommerce platforms along with ensuring proper site integration with fulfillment centers, call centers, and other systems. </li> <li>Ensure that Mars Symbioscience is a leader in analyzing and optimization of our web sites to ensure a leading edge user experience and conversion rates. We need to know where the user is going, why, and how to convert traffic in to sales in the most efficient way. </li> <li>Implementing leading edge technologies in to our web sites to increase product awareness, create web communities, and generate product sales. </li> <li>Work with SEM &amp; development vendors to ensure that our sites are maximizing our investments in SEM and working with our business partners, as well as sharing best practices &amp; knowledge across business segments. </li> <li>Manage the complete end to end business system, including back office systems, call center solutions and interfacing with our financial System Microsoft Dynamics NAV. </li> <li>Through successful management of third party vendors, build a leading edge ecommerce environment that allows Mars Symbioscience to quickly and efficiently deploy new web sites for new businesses. </li> </ul> <p><strong>Mission Critical Competencies </strong></p> <ul> <li>Business Acumen </li> <li>Creativity </li> <li>Dealing with Ambiguity </li> <li>Customer Focus </li> <li>Learning on the fly </li> <li>Problem Solving </li> <li>Process Management </li> <li>Managing and Measuring Work</li> </ul> <p><strong>Key Functional Skills/Knowledge</strong></p> <p><em>What does an associate have to be able to do &amp; optimization decisions.</li> <li>Manage and direct requests for system enhancements and support.</li> <li>Manage vendors responsible for development &amp; Web hosting. </li> <li>Manage all DNS, registrar info, Email, web site administration for Symbioscience sites.</li> <li>Microsoft Dynamics NAV experience is a plus. </li> </ul> <p><strong>Key Experiences</strong></p> <p><em>What professional experiences (assignments/ time in roles) must they have had and for what length of time in order to create superior results?</em></p> <ul> <li>7+ years experience with web business systems development &amp; Support </li> <li>7+ years leading/managing web development projects including ecommerce. </li> <li>3-7 years in web development / programming</li> <li>3-7 years working with offshore/remote development teams</li> <li>Working knowledge of PCI requirements </li> </ul> <p><strong>Desired Education</strong></p> <ul> <li>- Bachelors degree in Information Systems, Computer Science, Engineering, or related field</li> </ul> <p><strong>Decision Making Authority and Impact</strong></p> <p><em>Decisions and their Impact to the Business (What decisions will this associate be expected to make and at what risk to the business? </em></p> <p>Responsible for support strategy for web environment</p> <p>Responsible for online systems architecture</p> <p>Responsible for a yearly spend of approx 100-300k for web development activities.</p> <p><em>Recommendations</em> <em>(What recommendations will this associate be expected to make, to whom and to what impact to the business)</em></p> <p>Responsible for recommending web/ecommerce strategy &amp;
