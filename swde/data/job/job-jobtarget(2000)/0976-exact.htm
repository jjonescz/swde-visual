									<div id="jt_job_description">
										
											
												<p><span style="color: blue;"><strong>PROVIDER RELATIONS FIELD AGENTS<br />Miami, FL</strong></span><br /><br />Reporting to the Provider Relations Director, the successful candidate will work from the Miami office and be responsible for both the negotiation of physician/provider contracts and ongoing education/service to the physician/provider network in assigned market areas.<br /><br /><strong>Specifically, the Provider Relations Field Agent will:</strong></p> <ul> <li>Conduct quarterly audit for each line of business and provider directory category to determine ongoing network needs and provide written network developmental goals.</li> <li>Identify, on a semiannual basis, opportunities for re-negotiation of contracts by line of business and provider category and written re-negotiation goals.</li> <li>Negotiate contracts within approved rate reimbursement arrangements unless approved by the Provider Relations Director.</li> <li>Negotiate out-of-network contracts and rate, maintain a log of these negotiations, determine the ongoing need for the physician/provider and conduct negotiations for a contract (if appropriate).</li> <li>Negotiate rates that will achieve yearly department goals for Medical Loss Ratios (MRL).</li> <li>Upon completion of negotiated contract, be responsible for obtaining correctly completed credentialing applications and supporting documents.</li> <li>Upon approval by the Credentialing Committee, conduct New Office Orientation programs for all new primary care providers.</li> <li>Utilizing service guidelines, maintain a network stability as it relates to physician/provider termination's and panel closing by primary care physicians.</li> <li>Develop and implement, through service visits, educational tools for physicians/providers and their practice staff.</li> <li>Work closely, in assigned markets, with the Marketing Department staff and primary care physician practices to achieve/exceed member growth projections.</li> <li>Conduct monthly service-educational visits to primary care physician offices with more than 100 enrolled members and every-other-month visits to primary care physician offices with fewer than 100 enrolled members.</li> <li>Conduct monthly service-educational visits to high volume/key specialty offices as identified by the Provider Relations Director.</li> <li>Manage provider demographic information changes (tax identification numbers, new addresses etc.) and resolve identified discrepancies.</li> <li>In conjunction with the Provider Relations Director, develop and implement strategies for the reduction of Medical Loss Ratios (MLR) in identified primary care physician practices.</li> <li>Abide by all compliance requirements for the Department of Insurance (DOI), Agency for Health Care Administration (AHCA) and the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) as these apply to the Provider Relations Department activities.</li> </ul> <p><br /><br />At <strong>Freedom Health</strong>, we continue to provide the best quality, cost-effective benefits to serve our Medicare and Medicaid clients - and the most rewarding career opportunities to our team members - as we have since our founding. Our reputation for excellence has been recognized by Inc. magazine's "Top 500," which ranked us # 1 in Tampa-St. Pete-Clearwater, and by HealthMetrix Research Inc., which conferred their "Senior Choice Gold Award" upon us for the past 4 years. Our rapid growth has created great opportunities for you!</p>
											
										
										
										
											<br><b>Job Requirements</b><br>
											
												<p><strong>Qualifications include:</strong></p> <ul> <li>An undergraduate degree from an accredited college/university is preferred and at least three years of experience in the managed care industry with a focus on contracting. </li> <li>Ability to travel 50percent of the time</li> <li>Ability to navigate and master proprietary software programs. </li> <li>Ability to read and interpret documents.</li> <li>Excellent verbal/written communication skills with good grammar, voice and diction.</li> <li>Proficient in MS Office.</li> <li>Basic computer and keyboarding skills. </li> <li>Ability to formulate and articulate persuasive dialogue and to understand and convey complex financial information to providers.</li> </ul> <p><br /><br /><strong>In addition to an excellent compensation/benefits package, Freedom Health provides a vehicle and cell phone allowance. <br /><br />Please send resume indicating Job Code PRR-MIA in the subject line to: <span style="color: blue;">fhjobs@freedomh.com</span></strong></p> <li>eoe </li> <li>m/f/d/v </li> <li>dfwp</li>
