												<div><strong>Position Summary</strong></div> <div>&nbsp;</div> <div> <div> <div>Reporting to the Director, Security Intelligence, the Senior Intelligence Analyst is responsible for providing leadership, support, guidance and mentoring to the Security Intelligence Analysis Team. He/she will provide and assess intelligence analysis in order to identify threats, quantify vulnerabilities, and reduce risk to the corporation. The Senior Intelligence Analyst will focus on threats originating in information technology environments, and will utilize his/her expertise to ensure the production of actionable information, expressed in a clear and concise manner.&nbsp; In addition to expertise in the collection and analysis of intelligence, the ideal candidate will possess a strong understanding of the Information Security function and will be able to communicate fluently with IT Security specialists and SMEs. He/she will possess an understanding of the threats, current and emerging, that can face a public utility on both the enterprise and infrastructure levels. Exceptional communication and presentation skills will be backed by an ability to defend analytical conclusions in logical and persuasive terms. The Senior Intelligence Analyst will be able to work to tight deadlines and be able to function effectively in crisis situations, providing calm leadership to his/her team.&nbsp;</div> &nbsp;</div> </div> <div><strong>Department Overview</strong></div> <div>&nbsp;</div> <div> <div>Pacific Gas &amp; Electric Company, incorporated in California in 1905, is one of largest combination natural gas and electric utilities in the United States, delivering safe, reliable and responsive gas and electric service to approximately 15 million people across a 70,000 square mile area of northern and central California. Its service area stretches from Eureka in the north to Bakersfield in the south, and from the Sierra Nevada in the east to the Pacific Ocean. Based in San Francisco, the company has approximately 20,000 employees &nbsp;</div> <div>&nbsp;</div> <div>Pacific Gas and Electric Company is a wholly owned subsidiary of <a href="http://www.pgecorp.com/">PG&amp;E Corporation</a>. In 2009 PG&amp;E had approximately $40.8 billion in assets and generated revenues of approximately $14.6 billion.&nbsp;</div> <div>&nbsp;</div> <div>The Information Security and Compliance (IS&amp;C) function is responsible for IT security across the organization as well as the related implementation of appropriate controls for regulations such as SOX, HIPAA, CA1386 and NERC/CIP.
