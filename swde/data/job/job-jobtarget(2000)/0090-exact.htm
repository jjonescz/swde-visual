UCR gives every student the resources to explore, engage, imagine and excel. <br /><br />UCR is ranked 43rd among top public universities (US News and World Report 2010). <br /><br />At UC Riverside we celebrate diversity and are proud of our #5 ranking among the nations for most diverse universities (US News and World Report 2010). 											<br><b>Job Requirements</b><br>
											
												<p><strong>Minimum Requirements</strong>:</p> <li>Bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering or related field and/or an equivalent combination of education and experience. </li> <li>The ability to analyze the feasibility of engineering designs. </li> <li>The ability to design, fabricate, and test scientific apparatus, instrumentation systems, devices, and processes including those which must withstand extreme pressures, temperatures, and forces. </li> <li>Demonstrated ability to act as an engineering design consultant with knowledge of the design of standard machine components, material selection, tolerance selection, design for manufacturability/assemblability, etc. The ability to provide advice on the design of complex engineered devices including establishing design requirements. </li> <li>The ability to transform designs and concepts expressed through oral instructions, written instructions, sketches, and/or blueprints into completed projects. </li> <li>Possess judgment, ingenuity, and problem-solving skills necessary to complete a variety of open-ended design and fabrication tasks, both independently and with guidance from a supervisor. </li> <li>Proficiency in the use of computer-aided design and manufacturing (CAD/CAM) software such as SolidWorks, MasterCam, Surfcam, etc. </li> <li>Operating knowledge of materials used to construct scientific laboratory apparatus, instruments, devices, and processes, including knowledge of hardness, toughness, strength, welding characteristics, machining characteristics, corrosion resistance, etc., and how these characteristics influence material selection. The ability to select appropriate machining processes for a given material. </li> <li>Demonstrated knowledge and skill in the safe operation of a machine shop. Ability to safely test scientific apparatus, instrumentation systems, devices, and processes, including those which must withstand extreme pressures, temperatures, and forces. Knowledge of techniques and procedures for safe handling of various materials. </li> <li>Skill and experience in using traditional (manual) machining equipment including, but not limited to, drill presses, band saws, mills, and lathes. </li> <li>Skill and experience in using CNC machining equipment including multi-axis mills and lathes and five-axis wire EDM machines.
