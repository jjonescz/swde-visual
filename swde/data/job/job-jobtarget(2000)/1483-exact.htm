follows through to ensure issues are satisfactorily resolved. <br /><br />Maintains a well-informed knowledge base of competitors&rsquo; activities throughout assigned territory; informs manager of any changing competitive pricing programs or marketing directions. <br /><br />Attends and actively contributes to sales meetings and training programs. <br /><br />Complete all necessary paperwork to support sales activities including sales orders, sales agreements, billing forms and sales reports in a manner consistent with quality control guidelines.<br /><br />Collect and account for funds, collect equipment and provide receipts according to company policy and guidelines.&nbsp; <br /><br />May perform certain technical functions such as installation of digital boxes, activation/disconnection of existing cable lines and installation of telephone MTA&rsquo;s when requested by the company and when trained in the correct procedure.</span></p>
