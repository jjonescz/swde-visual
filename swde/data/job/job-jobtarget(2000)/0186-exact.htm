												<p><strong>Company Description</strong>: Bison Gear and Engineering Corporation is a rapidly growing manufacturer of electric motors and gearmotors located in a far west suburb of Chicago with over 200 employees and aggressively growing top line sales. Products manufactured are used in a variety of equipment serving markets in food service, packaging, medical, machine tool, agriculture and industrial machinery. We have an immediate need for a <strong>Electrical Design Engineer I </strong>. <br /><br /><strong>Primary Responsibilities:</strong> Applicant will be primarily responsible for assisting in the design and documentation of Bison manufactured AC electric motor product offerings that comply with customer and application engineering requested specifications. They will monitor competitive motor products, announcements &amp; activities, and recommends improvement projects &amp; cost reduction programs to enhance Bison's motor product lines.<br /><br /><strong>Typical duties include</strong>:</p> <ul> <li>Provides the necessary electrical and mechanical expertise to design and apply existing AC electric motor product lines from specifications to production.</li> <li>Generates, processes, checks, and/or resolves Engineering Prototypes (EPs), New Unit Releases (NURs), Engineering Change Notices (ECNs), Engineering Deviation Notifications (EDNs), Returned Goods Authorizations (RGAs), Deviation Reports (DRs), and Corrective Action Reports (CARs) related to Bison's AC motor products as necessary.</li> <li>May act as Project Manager on product cost reduction programs and product improvement projects for AC electric motor designs and concepts.</li> <li>Directs Life Tests on Bison AC motors to improve product life both as requested and where necessary, and reports test results.</li> <li>Is responsible for maintaining project/program schedules and regularly reports progress.</li> <li>Helps generate and operates within annually prepared budgets for accounts, projects, and programs.</li> <li>Is responsible for the maintenance of all files, operating procedures, calculation techniques, computer programs, and indexes related to the effective performance of the job.</li> <li>Must be able to program an IBM-compatible computer in a currently supported engineering programming language; and may be required to write software to support calculation procedures to be used by others within the organization.</li> <li>Will work with Lab Technicians, Design Drafters, and other Engineers in the Electrical Engineering and Mechanical Design Groups as necessary.</li> <li>Is responsible for generating clear, concise instructions to Laboratory and Drafting personnel.</li> <li>Reviews technical publications, competitive literature, etc. to maintain an awareness of competitive products and activities, and regularly reports findings.</li> <li>Directs competitive analysis testing of products from other manufacturers as necessary, and generates appropriate competitive analysis reports.</li> <li>Stays abreast of "state-of-the-art" technological advancements, new processing techniques, and new materials; and regularly reports findings.</li> <li>Recommends product improvements, cost reductions, and production methods to enhance Bison products, their life expectancy, and quality.</li> <li>Contributes ideas and suggestions for the creation of new products and proposes associated development projects/programs.</li> <li>Provides engineering assistance to other members of the organization as directed.</li> <li>May also provide technical support to other members of the engineering staff.</li> <li>Performs other duties and functions as may be directed from time to time by his/her supervisor.</li> </ul> <p><br /><br /><br /></p>
											
										
										
										
											<br><b>Job Requirements</b><br>
											
												<p><strong>Qualifications</strong>: Qualified applicants must have BSEE with Power Option or related four-year College Degree from an accredited College/University. They must have knowledge of AC induction Motor design and application. AC motor design and/or development experience is a plus. Zero to four years of related experience is preferred. The ability to program an IBM compatible computer in Visual Basic or other engineering language; and write software to support calculation procedures used by others within the organization is necessary. You must exhibit excellent organization, communication and documentation skills and possess the capability to work independently while managing multiple projects with competing deadlines.<br /><br /><strong>Response Information</strong>: Bison Gear offers a highly competitive salary, comprehensive benefit plan as well as a smoke free and business casual work environment, tuition reimbursement, and an on-site fitness center. <strong>We are a drug-free workplace. </strong>Local candidates are encouraged to apply, as there is no relocation allowance for this position.
