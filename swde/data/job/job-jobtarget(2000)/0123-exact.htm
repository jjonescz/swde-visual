												<p><strong>POSITION 2010-85421-04973</strong></p> <p>The clinical applications manager is responsible for planning and organizing all clinical applications related to Harrison Medical Center's electronic medical record.&nbsp; He/She is a clinician who is knowledgeable in standardizing clinical practices thru the use of an EMR and its effect on patient care within the inpatient and outpatient departments and its staff.&nbsp; The Manager will oversee a team of analysts in the implementation and ongoing support of all clinical applications including, but not limited to GE/MUSE EKG, Interfaced and Non-interfaced Orders and Results, worklists, Sunrise Pharmacy, Records Manager, Emergency Department tracking board, charges, Endotool, Interchange and Computerized Physician Order Entry (CPOE). The Clinical Applications Manager will report to the Director of Clinical Information Systems.</p> <p>Additionally, the manager will be responsible for and/or possess knowledge in the following:</p> <ul> <li>Be familiar with organizational policies, procedures, business operations, and regulatory requirements relating to electronic clinical applications. </li> <li>Be familiar with national standards related to quality and safety. </li> <li>Maintain strict change control with all database environments</li> <li>Participate in the development of policies and procedures related electronic clinical applications </li> <li>Participate in the organization's meetings with clinical leaders where decisions will be made related to content, workflow and clinical practice overall</li> <li>Work closely with his/her counterpart, the Clinical Documentation Manager in ensuring alignment of orders and clinical documentation content.</li> </ul> <p><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">MAJOR DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</span></strong><br /><br />A representative summary of tasks to be performed by the Manager is provided below. The Manager may be asked to perform job-related tasks other than those specifically stated in the description. The duties and responsibilities of the position are to be carried out in a manner that is consistent with mission and values of Harrison Medical Center.</p>
											
										
										
										
											<br><b>Job Requirements</b><br>
											
												<p>Required Qualifications:</p> <ul> <li>Bachelor's degree from accredited university or college in the Health Sciences (Nursing, Medicine, etc.). </li> <li>Clinical or operational experience in inpatient clinical practice </li> <li>Demonstrated proficiency in project management </li> <li>Proficiency in database change management</li> <li>Superior workflow process analysis and design </li> <li>Strong assertiveness skills, ability to manage conflict in a variety of situations </li> <li>Effective leadership and mentorship skills and strong customer service orientation </li> <li>Superior task, organizational and people management skills </li> <li>Strong communication and interpersonal skills </li> <li>Demonstrated ability to align and motivate key process stakeholders, including nurses, physicians, and other clinical/administrative staff </li> <li>Demonstrated ability to interact with interdisciplinary teams </li> <li>Strong skills in MS Office software Preferred Qualifications </li> <li>Experience with implementing clinical information systems and/or large-scale and complex standardization and implementation of an EMR &nbsp;</li> <li>Demonstrated clinical systems implementation skills </li> <li>Minimum 3 years management experience<br /><br />Key Duties and Responsibilities:<br /><br />The Manager coordinates the planning and organization of clinical applications content development. In addition to the duties discussed above, the Manager is responsible for the following: </li> <li>General knowledgeable and understanding of clinical applications and its functionality</li> <li>Plans, organizes and facilitates processes to support clinicians in development of clinical applications, to include CPOE</li> <li>Plans, organizes and facilitates processes to support all clinical staff and disciplines as it relates to the clinical applications supported</li> <li>Assesses content implementation strategies and the impact those strategies will have on workflows and operations </li> <li>Documents the current and future state workflows that are impacted by clinical applications </li> <li>Create current state and future state workflow development, application design and development, content issues resolution, implementation and ongoing support. </li> <li>Understands how content and functionality affect practice, workflow, and operations </li> <li>Contributes to policy and procedure development that will be used in conjunction with the clinical applications </li> <li>Achieves in-depth knowledge of the software </li> <li>Conducts regular day-to-day communication with content vendors; works with vendors to tailor content to organizational needs </li> <li>Contributes to practice workflow analysis and redesign </li> <li>Acts as a contact and coordinator for questions and issues that arise related to clinical content </li> <li>Troubleshoots workflow issues or questions from users </li> <li>Participates in regular status reporting </li> <li>Prepares detailed content specifications as needed </li> <li>Collects information regarding potential system enhancement needs related to content </li> <li>Supervises and mentors assigned analyst </li> <li>Other duties as assigned </li> </ul>
