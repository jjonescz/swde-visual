												<p>Hawai'i Natural History Association (HNHA) seeks a full-time I.T. Systems Administrator at its headquarters located in Hawai'i Volcanoes National Park. Travel to other locations may be required.<br /><br />HNHA is a nonprofit organization and National Park Service Cooperating Association. We inspire the discovery and stewardship of the natural and cultural heritage of Pacific Island parks.<br /><br />POSITION DESCRIPTION:<br /><br />Duties include, but are not limited to:</p> <ul> <li> Manage HNHA's point of sale system (presently eight sales outlets)</li> <li> Manage all hardware, software, and network functionality in our operations at headquarters, four additional Hawai'i Island parks, and Haleakal&auml; NP on Maui </li> <li> Evaluate software and hardware needs; take the lead in installation and implementation </li> <li> Troubleshoot, diagnose and resolve hardware and software issues. </li> <li> Coordinate and oversee contract information technology specialists as needed.</li> <li> Design, implement and maintain computer data backup plan and system; help establish policies and procedures related to corporate network/server management</li> <li> Coordinate and implement computer-based communications technology such as FTP, VPN, etc.</li> <li> Develop and help conduct employee computer training program</li> <li> Work with staff and service providers to design and implement website</li> <li> Assists with site maintenance, feature upgrades, and related e-media</li> <li> Assist or manage other projects as needed</li> </ul> <p>WAGES/BENEFITS:<br /> Starting Wage:&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;$ 18.16&nbsp; per hour<br /> Starting Date:&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;December, 2010<br /> Benefits:&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;Medical (inc. drug, dental, vision); paid holiday, vacation and sick leave; employee retirement plan<br /> <br /> HOW TO APPLY:<br /> <br /> Deadline:&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;November 19, 2010 (deadline may be extended until suitable candidate is selected.)<br /> <br /> <br /> Submit cover letter, application and r&eacute;sum&eacute; to:<br /> <br /> I.T. Systems Administrator<br /> Hawaii Natural History Association<br /> P. O. Box 74<br /> Hawaii National Park, HI&nbsp; 96718<br /> <br /> Information may also be submitted via fax to (808) 985-7333 <br /> or via e-mail to hawaiinha@yahoo.com</p>
											
										
										
										
											<br><b>Job Requirements</b><br>
											
												<p><br />MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS AND NECESSARY SKILLS:</p> <ul> <li> Bachelor's degree in computer science or related field</li> <li> Knowledge of Mac platform hardware, software, and networks (LAN and WAN)</li> <li> Three years previous experience and/or training that includes personal computer use, network administration, technical support, preferably in a Mac environment</li> <li> Knowledge of computerized point of sale systems</li> <li> Knowledge of website design and e-media</li> <li> General office administration experience </li> <li> Ability to keep current on new technologies and industry trends</li> <li> Flexibility to respond to emergency infrastructure/network servers issues outside of standard business hours</li> <li> Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, and able to manage multiple priorities and work in a fast-paced environment</li> <li> Ability to work with a variety of people in a positive, productive manner</li> <li> Exhibits behavior and performs job in a manner which supports HNHA's vision and values, teamwork, excellence, attitude, and mutual respect, as well as cooperation, responsibility, initiative and leadership</li> </ul>
