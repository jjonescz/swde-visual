									<div id="jt_job_description">
										
											
												<p style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt;">&nbsp;</p> <p><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Job Description</span></strong></p> <p>&nbsp;The Comptroller is the primary financial manager for the organization and is responsible for ensuring that all financial records of the organization are maintained accurately; preparing budgets and analyses of organizational finances; ensuring that all payments, taxes, and other disbursements are made in an accurate and timely manner; and supervising the Bookkeeper. He or she tracks and analyzes employee timesheets, is the lead staff member for our staff financial team, works with the Board treasurer, and ensures that the internal controls are followed.</p> <p>&nbsp;The Comptroller is an hourly, part-time position of approximately 20 hours per week, working Monday - Friday.&nbsp;</p> <p>&nbsp;<strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Responsibilities</span></strong></p> <p>&nbsp;1) <strong>The Comptroller is responsible for ensuring that all financial records of the organization are maintained accurately, preparing budgets and analyses of finances, and ensuring that all payments, taxes, and other disbursements are made in an accurate and timely manner. </strong>Duties include:</p> <ul> <li>reviewing and verifying the accuracy of monthly, quarterly, and annual financial statements prepared by the Bookkeeper;</li> <li>serving as a primary contact for all banks and brokerage houses that Northern Plains has accounts with;</li> <li>ensuring that the reconciliations of all checking, savings, endowment, and brokerage house accounts are completed on a monthly basis;</li> <li>ensuring that Northern Plains' Internal Controls are followed; </li> <li>ensuring that all internal and external reports are completed in a timely manner;</li> <li>preparing Northern Plains' annual budget for approval and monitoring the budget on a monthly basis;</li> <li>preparing financial analyses, such as cash-flow projections, on a regular basis for the Staff Director;</li> <li>ensuring that all tax reports (worker's compensation, payroll reports and federal Form 990) are properly prepared and submitted by the deadlines and responding to any questions about our 990 report;</li> <li>working with the outside auditor to ensure that the annual audit is completed;</li> <li>coding all bills for the Bookkeeper;</li> <li>ensuring the payment of all accounts payable, as authorized, and accounts receivable; </li> <li>ensuring that Northern Plains' insurance coverage (auto, building, other) is current and appropriate;</li> <li>ensuring approved annual organizational contributions to employee pension plans are distributed in a timely and accurate manner, and reports completed;</li> <li>ensuring that gifts of stock and other non-cash gifts (<em>e.g</em>., automobiles, office equipment) are accurately recorded and dealt with appropriately;</li> <li>analyzing employee timesheets and providing monthly reports to the Staff Director of the number of hours worked, project by project, for each employee;</li> <li>tracking employee time off, sick leave, and alternative transportation credits;</li> <li>ensuring appropriate Personnel Policy guidelines are adhered to and followed;</li> <li>serving as the Lead for the Staff Financial Team; and,</li> <li>serving as the staff contact for the Board's Endowment Investment Committee and for the Board Treasurer.</li> </ul> <p>&nbsp;2) <strong>The Comptroller supervises the Bookkeeper</strong>.
