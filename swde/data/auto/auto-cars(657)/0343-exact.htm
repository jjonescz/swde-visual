Vehicle Summary

The Mini Cooper Countryman serves as the brand's entry into the small-crossover segment. It has a taller stance and, unlike other Coopers, four conventional side doors and available all-wheel drive. Buyers have a choice of a normally aspirated four-cylinder engine or a turbo four-cylinder (S versions). It's scheduled to hit U.S. dealerships in early 2011, and it competes against the Volkswagen Tiguan and Volvo XC60.  Read more
